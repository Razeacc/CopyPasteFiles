Idea: Fourth Marshal will be an Indie Psychological Narrative RPG Gacha Game that explores the idea of Fourth Marshals, people who work towarda maintaining the separation of reality and fiction.
The game has you play as one of these "supposed" Fourth Marshals as you work alongside Kaitzar and his AI Assistant Razi-Chan. Together, you help teach at Santa Besado Institute to mentor the next generation of Fourth Marshals (as they don't necessarily have to be people from our real world). To do so, you use a device called the Alternate Fiction, which simulates different past time periods in the Scripted Fates canon, allowing you to try and explore these events to pinpoint "what most likely happened" while also using it to just... fuck around, and "see what would happen". The game will heavily focus on, and deal with, themes of being delusional over fictional stories (simulations), knowing when you've been driven too far into fantasy, how love for fiction can inspire people to become better... or worse, etc. It will also feature a highly-complex "Psychology Stat" system, rather than typical gacha games with typical Crit Chance stats, Crit Damage stats, etc.
