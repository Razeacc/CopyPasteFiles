# OVERVIEW
After real-life months of buildup, the Stone Society has managed to create, test, and polish a fully-working Hot Air Balloon. It was even modified using some Control-Powered sails in its four quadrants, some leftover tech from The Long Shush courtesy of Besado. Ode to HER Firmata follows the Take-off Carnival as the Stone Society finaly celebrates as they send a group of brave volunteers to The Spire through the hot air balloon, all in the hopes that this journey will finally answer all their problems and questions.
For Locals, they hope that's where their memories from before The Spirepocalypse are held.
For Otherworlders, they hope it'll be a gateway or bridge that'll take them home.

### Making B.I.I.G. Moves!
Here's how Event RPs will work here in Firmata Frontier moving forward, and why each major event will be called an "Episode".
Ode to HER Firmata will first release as a LIVE Roleplay event!

The additional music, artwork, cutscenes, and animation I promised will be produced afterward as part of a Full Low-Poly 3D Motion Picture release.
-# (Think of this as a "TV vs BlueRay release" some animes have)

Players who make B.I.I.G. Moves
-# (Bibliographically Impossible-to-Ignore Gambits)
during the Live RP will likely have their actions, achievements, or even their character/s represented in the Motion Picture release.
BIIG Moves are actions that push the story forward or challenge it slightly~! They're moments that matter. Decisions, sacrifices, failures, victories, discoveries, and acts of creativity that leave a lasting impact on the narrative.
Most importantly, have fun and don't be afraid to take risks~!
YOU, and your characters matter here too.

Want a guaranteed BIIG Move before the event begins?
Consider creating a Carnival Booth. Booths will become permanent fixtures of "Newpoint Village", or whatever's left of it.

For more questions or details about the event, feel free to skin through the buttons down below.
*May fate show mercy to thee~*

### Live RP Overview
The LIVE Roleplay release is the first version of an Episode, raw and barely-filtered. This is where the story is experienced directly through roleplay with other roleplayers and Narrators.
Happenings unfold in real time and may be influenced by player decisions, interactions, and BIIG Moves.
The Motion Picture release is built from what happens here, treating it as a framework/guide. It's like a storyboard for the narrative.

Think of the LIVE Roleplay release as experiencing history while it's still being written.

### Motion Picture Overview
Following the LIVE Roleplay, a Motion Picture Animation adaptation will be planned and put into production for the next few months. The "script" in these animated episodes will likely depict characters, locations, and moments from the LIVE Roleplay session.

These adaptations may include original music, artwork, cutscenes, animation, voice acting(?), narration, additional scene direction, and additional foreshadowing/clues.
If LIVE Roleplay acts as an initial investigation, the Motion Picture functions as the deep-dive analysis on a storyline.

The artstyle will follow a Low-Poly 3D asthetic using the Unity Engine to handle things like procedural environments/particles and immersive audio. While this means setting up and starting production (especially for the first episode) may take much longer than normal, once all the sets and pieces are ready, future episode production will be significantly faster.
DISCLAIMER: This is all planned. The actual final product may be more or less of these "planned" specifications. Nothing here is promised, merely hoped-for and aimed towards.

### Carnival Booth Overview
Want your character to leave a mark before the first Script Recalculation begins?

The Take-Off Carnival is accepting player-made booths and attractions!

Every approved booth becomes a permanent fixture of Newpoint Village after the event, allowing your character to leave a lasting mark on the setting before the story changes.

### REWARDS
Every booth can award 🖋️ Scripting Ink to booth owners and participants. This event currency is used to obtain special limited-time, event-exclusive rewards.
-# (Shameless FOMO)

Additional in-character prizes are allowed if they make sense for your game.
However, in a world where currency has yet to be invented, the greatest reward may simply be bragging rights.

### GUIDELINES
• Any Verified RPer may host a booth.
• NPCs may host booths.
• You may create multiple booths.
• Booths should fit the Take-Off Carnival theme.
• Games should be simple and easy for visitors to understand.

### LIMITATIONS
• Booths should remain compatible with Stone Society-era technology.
• Electronics and modern machinery are not allowed unless justified (somehow).
• Materials needed for the booth should be easy to gather unless explained/reasonable
-# (IE: If a booth uses lots of gems, the booth organizer must be tired during the event due to having spent the past few days finding the gems, leading to potential foreshadowing opportunities).
• Narrators may request adjustments if a booth would conflict with the event.
