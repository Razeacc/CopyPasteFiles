# Overview
The main setting of Beyond Eternity, The Null Crossing acts as the purgatory for everything that has  been "sentienced" to cease to exist (annulment).
After being declared for annulment, they get sent here where they slowly pass onto Entropy.
For all souls, however, they're collected by Nul Detta and stored within his hivemind, akin to a Grim Reaper "not doing his job".

The Null Crossing is home to Nul Detta, The Innocent Prince. Metaphore of Psyche, and Multiversal Hivemind of souls that have ceased to exist. THEY are where all caradel souls come from, who masterminds the vicars of Null, and who allows entropy to occasionally leak into reality.

## Time
Time works in a very messed up way in The Null Crossing as the Control Barrier atop it acts akin to an early prototype version of a Firmata. If considered as such, this would mean The Null Crossing is the largest Firmata in all of fiction.
Time is utterly distorted here, with it oftentimes stopping, speeding up, moving backwards, or even resetting randomly from time to time. In fact, following the events of Beyond Eternity, when Khueen attempted to break out of The Null, The 4th Beyond pushed the Null Crossing back in time so far, they went back to the time of The Spirepocalypse, essentially "resyncing it" back to where we currently are in the present.

## Space
As for space, this is where even Ulhysses' attempt at a "Firmata" far exceeds that of even modern day Firmatas. The Control Barrier separating The Null Crossing from The Infinite Fields and the rest of The Worlds Aplenty, both compresses and expands The Null Crossing across the whole of the infinite fields.
Space is so distorted here, despite how small the Null Crossing actually is (roughly The Locunex Region alone), digging far enough down across any location in The Infinite Fields will somehow land you somewhere in The Null Crossing. This compressed-expansion is what further weakens the Null Crossing from its prime state long ago.

Despite that weakening, it is still just enough to act as the true final barrier between the "true hell" of this world.
Endless Entropy.
