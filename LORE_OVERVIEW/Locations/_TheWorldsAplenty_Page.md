# Mechanics of The Worlds Aplenty
## Time
An invisible property called "timelight" flows out from the Timelines in the Timeline Sky. Timelight ignores physical barriers as it's what allows time to flow and occur within The Worlds Aplenty, with it only being somewhat blocked by the Spirite Leylines and Control Barrier, resulting in The Null Crossing having wildly-desynchronized time flow.
This is partially why it gets hotter when you approach timelines, as your body literally moves faster through time, heating up more than you're used to, while also aging you slightly faster than normal.
This phenomena is also why Firmatas are so complicated, as they entirely block out timelight by default in order to simulate an actual sky. Translating timelight into "artificial timelight" within the Firmata, or letting it flow through the firmata, is incredibly difficult— with the later being akin to a machine that automatically threads moving needles.

## Cardinal Directions
*   **North and South** - These sides extend infinitely. It is unknown truly how far they go.
*   **West** - This side can potentially extend endlessly, but you'll eventually step into Vektat's Valley, which means you should step back as soon as possible before you either go beyond into the 2nd dimension, or Vektat flies overhead and accidentally sends you into spatial limbo.
*   **East** - Go far enough east, and you'll simply reach The 4th's Wall. Travel along the central exis, and you'll even find the Eastern Edge and Avalon City itself.
*   **Up** - High above the world is the timeline sky, where universes exist... and most importantly, Timeliners. Go beyond even that, and you get Unknow Space, literally.
*   **Down** - Digging into the earth and through the veins of spirite leylines, you'll eventually hit the Control Barrier. Break through even that, and you get to The Null Crossing, purgatory of Entropy. Go through even that, and you reach the other end of this world, Endless Entropy.

## Date Tracking
Time (months/days/years/hours/minutes, etc.) is synced perfectly with how our real world time measurements are scaled. This is due to the AoS and Archkin influence, taking direct inspiration from our real world. Higher-up AoS members, and previous Archgod rulers, were ware of The 4th Beyond's (our) role in this world as spectators. Thus, they manipulated their societies to follow 12-month calendars, 24-hour days, 60-second minutes, 30/31/28-day months, etc. This genuinely makes it more difficult for them, but they adapted and adjusted overtime. They even came up with their own fake stories to tell the masses to explain why time is organized as such, with barely anyone knowing it's organized like our time measurement systems specifically for our convenience.

## Spatial Dimensions
These are the physical manifestations of each dimension, dictating where certain stories take-place. Spatial Dimensions are determined by story-content, not artstyle.
Even if a story is animated in 2D, if it utilizes depth in its world in any way, it's considered a 3rd-Dimensional Story.
From the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and even the 4th dimension, they all have certain uncharted boundaries and sections in The Worlds Aplenty. Vektat's Valley is specifically designed to keep and maintain these boundaries, as to ensure no dimension accidentally spills over into another, as such consequences can be quite disastrous.

As a recap of what each dimension is:
*   **1st Dimension** follows merely the X axis. It is a single point in space. Despite its small size, Vektat has dedicated a specialized 2-dimensional Firmata around it specifically ensure he has its constant direct attention on it at all times, as the 1st dimension spreading would result in massive spatial distortions of unimaginable proportions.
*   **2nd Dimension** is the second-largest dimension behind the 3rd... technically speaking. It follows the X and Y axis, allowing horizontal and vertical movement. Although some stories follow this dimension, due to the rules established previously about 2D artstyles often still being in 3D worlds, the 2nd dimension isn't as large as you would've expected it to be. Still, it's large enough for Vektat's Valley to be immeasurable in-size, purely due to how an "infinitely-expanding" worlds works. The 2nd dimension is merely a smaller infinity than the 3rd dimension's infinity.
Speaking of-
*   **3rd Dimension** is where a vast majority of stories take place, adding depth to the equation. There's not much more to add here.
*   **4th Dimension** starts getting complicated, as it's technically the Timeline Sky. Rather than following the similar planar dimensions, the 4th Dimension overlaps atop all other dimensions as it utilizes time travel as a dimension in space. Since you can physically move up and down a outside timeline to move into the past or future in the universe within it, alongside being able to timetravel up and down a timeline to quickly move across the timeline sky, this is how it functions as the 4th dimension. Unlike the others, you can freely move within the 4th Dimension, as long as you remain atop your home dimension. Going to the 2nd Dimension within the 4th Dimension would still royally mess you up.
*   The **5th Dimension** does not exist, it is a lie... ok fine- it's rumored to be Decanoluzioned Fiction, but this cannot be confirmed currently.
*   The **6th Dimension** actually does not exist in the slightest, and there are no rumors about it at all... ok it might be the Bobuverse, but even that's already questionably-canon.

## Gravity
Gravity wells function very differently in The Worlds Aplenty, as there isn't some "intensly-high mass" object in the bottom of this world, especially as this is not a planet.
Instead, the infinite layer of Entropy beneath the Null Crossing acts as a constant pull, tugging everything in The Worlds Aplenty towards it at an acceleration of 9.8m/s, perfectly mimicing gravity from The 4th Beyond's world.
This also means compasses most likely would not work here. Instead, specialized compasses made by the AoS point to The 4th's Wall, indicating where East is, rather than North.

# The Theory of Reality Levels
According to discussions between Fourth Marshals and high-ranking AoS scientists, it's believed that the Fictional World of The Worlds Aplenty and our world of "reality" are merely "levels" in an exponentially-expanding pyramid called the "Levels of Reality".
For every person in our world capable of creating fiction, whenever they write fiction, they create a whole new "Worlds Aplenty".
Whenever someone from The Worlds Aplenty writes their own stories, they too create another layer of fiction below their own perceived layer of "reality".
As for us, if this theory holds weight, it implies that we too are merely the "fictional world" of a higher being's "reality". And that higher being is merely the fictional world of a being above it.

This is the Theory of Reality Levels.
