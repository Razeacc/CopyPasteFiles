Idea: An AoS-Managed school situation on an island in the bay of Cubiqua with a maglev connecting it to the city. It trains and teaches (indoctrinates) future AoS members, those wanting to become timeline hoppers, and even certain real-world skills and trades like welding or even business. It has a Highschool division, but is mainly known for its college courses due to how exclusive and "unique" they can be. This is the main setting of Fourth Marshal.
