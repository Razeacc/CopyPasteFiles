## Overview
The proximity of The 4th's Wall has historically attracted philosophers, explorers, religious movements, and extremely reckless scientists to examine the green-tinted prismatic rainbow walls that stretch infinitely north and south, and *almost* infinitely high up. Prior to The Spirepocalypse, all Spires actually pointed towards the "top" of The 4th's Wall.
It is located just beyond Avalon City.

## The Trek to Avalon
A major secret behind The 4th's Wall is directly connected to the newly-born archkin phenomena present in Exspanses within the eastern hemisplane of The Worlds Aplenty.
*   **First Confluence**
> From the top of The 4th's Wall, where The Court of The 4th resides, comets often shoot out from here, falling down to the ground. From those comets, newly-born archkin are fabricated into reality.

*   **Second Confluence**
> From that point on, these archkin children are lead by skribbles through exspanse to exspanse, gathering memories and growing stronger over time.

*   **Third Confluence**
> Eventually, they reach Avalon City, to where they climb down into its central crater. Here, they find an obscure tunnel that leads them to a massive chasm underneath Avalon City where The 4th's Wall has begun to crack and weather away.

*   **Final Confluence**
> Whenever writers "break" the 4th wall, it forms a crack on The 4th's Wall. These newborn archkin are tasked to find a section of the wall where they'd be "comfortable" slot themselves in, and become one with the wall, sacrificing all their acquired memories, and their life, to repair The 4th's Wall. This is what became of Archie following Farewell Avalon and Trek to Avalon.

The souls of these newborn archkin are then sent to The Null Crossing, where they join Dett Minor. But rather than completely assimilating to the hivemind, Dett lets them retain their individuality, knowing that the Null Crossing will open up again one day, letting these archkin properly cross over into the afterlife. This is something not even Nul Detta is aware of.
