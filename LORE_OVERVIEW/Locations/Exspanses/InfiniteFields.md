# Overview
Carrying Exspanses and the Aeonic Ocean, the Infinite Fields is a nearly-infinitely expansive world beneath the timeline sky where a nearly-unchartable level of exploration can be had in this vast open world. As this is also technically a "flat" world, some common phenomena in our world does not apply here whatsoever, such as Atmospheres being labels as Atmosplanes. Or world curvature being entirely non-existent, instead being replaced with "distant fog" which merely obscures structures once you get too far away from them.
