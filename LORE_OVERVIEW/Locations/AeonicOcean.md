# Overview
An infinitely-expansive ocean that surrounds every exspanse, it's almost immeasurably deep, with some sections even reachiong the Control Barrier itself. Some oceanic spires are possible here as well, if spirite leylines manage to erode through sections of abyssmal sections.

Due to a lack of tidal forces, all waves in the Aeonic Ocean, which includes other bodies of water like The Continent's Fracturing Sea, are relatively calm compared to how waves appear in our real world. **All wave phenomena is influenced by winds and winds alone.**

As for The Fracturing, due to how close The Null Crossing can be to the Aeonic Ocean, there are some pockets of slighlyt-higher Fracturing energy in the waters, creating low-intensity Fracturing zones out at sea. A good example of this is, once again, Lejos'/The Continent's Fracturing Sea at its center, which features a relatively high-concentration of Fracturing Energy from the seas alone, due to the events of The Spirepocalypse and Nul Detta's Plans before and during Firmata Frontier.

## Abyssmal Zones
These are deep and massive Fracturing Zones that seep into the waters of certain sections in the Aeonic Ocean. They host unique caradel such as Fissuring Megalodon, "abberant" caradels (which appear akin to massiva Magnapina squid), and the occasional Infinitus Caradel, which uses is unnaturally massive and long legs to crush the seafloor even further to spread the Abyssmal Zones more.
The AoS takes sightings of Infinitus Caradels very seriously, as there is currently now way to resonably "reclaim" land lost to Abussmal Zones due to Infinitus Caradel spread.
