Idea: The Worlds Aplenty is the true name of the overarching setting. It's split into 2 halves, The Timeline Sky and The Infinite Fields. The Timeline Sky is what makes up the "sky" of this world, endless pillars of light that contain countless universes within. When universes are active, they glow and bring the world an iconic blue sky. When universes experience heat death, they dim out and bring about the night sky. Timelies out of sync are instead active during the "night" producing subtle streaks of bright blue across the night sky, giving The Infinite Fields one of the most unique night skies in fiction... and hopefully one of the most unique takes of the concept of the multiverse as well.
Since this story only treats the Multiverse as a mere piece of its world, especially since characters from outside the multiverse / timeline sky (aka 99% of the cast) canonically do not have multiverse counterparts (they would instead technically have "omniverse" counterparts, but we don't tackle the omniverse in Scripted Fates, it's 100% entirely ignored and never taken into consideration nor treated as an actual idea). Furthermore, all locations listed here are in the Infinite Fields, unless stated otherwise.
