# Overview
A single blue pillar stretching across one end of infinity to another end of infinity, with infinite numbers of these pillars endlessly blanketting the sky of The Worlds Aplenty until barely anything can pass through the endless sea of universes; the Timeline Sky is one of the most distinctly unique features of The Worlds Aplenty. Rather than following other pop-culture ideas of Multiverses, such as "bubble universes", multiverses in The Worlds Aplenty's logic relies entirely on these physical timelines.
These timelines are also connecting to Spires, which travel through the timeline sky to bridge itself towards the top of The 4th's Wall. While piercing the sky of timelines, it transfers spirite towards these timelines, helping keep it fueld and powered endlessly.

## Day/Night Cycle
Timelines follow the "Pulsating Universe" theory. Whenever a timeline is "active", it glows a bright blue, giving daytime its iconic morning blue glow. However, as timelines shift towards its "heat death" phase, the timelines darken as the sky enters night. Unsynced timelines often glow bright and "awaken" during this time instead, creating the unique string-like blanket of "stars" across The Worlds Aplenty's night sky. Then, when the timeline shifts back to its "big bang" phase, timelines one by one turn bright blue once more, bringing back daytime.

The time cycle for timeline shifting between day and night syncs perfectly to how day/night cycles were in the Mediterranian during the prehistoric period. This is entirely due to Ulhysse and how she invented time in the first place.

## Voided Timelines
By completely destroying every single point of time within a timeline, the AoS is capable of entirely hollowing out a timeline to the point where it's filled with nothing but absolute nothingness.
These voided-out timelines are what they use to contain and restrain the most dangerous and powerful archgods for archose harvesting, interrogation, torture, or merely purely for imprisonment.

Morgale de Metso is currently inside one of these voided timelines.

## Navigation
This header will purely clarify and discuss how members of the AoS, or anyone from The Worlds Aplenty, can traverse across the Timeline Sky.

### Spirevators
These are essentially "space elevators" built alongside spires, they act similar to staircases that lead into subways. When constructed properly, anyone can use a Spirevator to travel up and down a Spire. Without it, travel is only possible by people "branded" by The 4th Beyond and protected by them. This is true either for pale green and cyan spires.
Archkin are only able to safely travel up spirevators and golden spires (which are slowly going extinct-)
Atop these Spirevators are stations known as Temporal Terminals.

### Temporal Terminals
These often connect to several different timelines in a subway station-like manner. They have entrypoints positioned in time periods where the timelines is entirely voided out, similar to Voided Timelines, to avoid any time-travelling mishaps.
In these stations, mechanized Script-Breaker Knives (WIP Name) are used to cut into timelines in a way where people can enter the time period. These knives are also used by the AoS to enter and leave timelines at will.

### Time Travel Lines
Each tiny ridge on a timeline functions as a single physical "second" (it's actually unclear what the exact time measurement is) in a moment within the timeline's universe. Entering and leaving a timeline from a different point, essentially allows you to time travel.
By entering timelines where time travel is possible, members of the AoS establish "time travel lines" across different timelines where people from the Infinite Fields can enter a timeline, travel to a specific time period, jump across branching timelines, and leave the timelines to end up in wildly-distant locations on The Worlds Aplenty. By jumping from time period to time period, and alternate universe to alternate universe, members originally from outside the timeline are able to essentially travel at "beyond light speed" in The Worlds Aplenty.
This same method is how The AoS originally attempted to send Reinforcements from Avalon City to Lejos right before The Spirepocalypse and Creation of Her Firmata.
