####		Firmata's Rolling System
........................................

The '''Firmata Rolling System''' is the universal resolution mechanic used across the '''[[Firmata Frontier]]''' roleplay server. It determines the '''difficulty''' of actions, the '''success''' of attempts, and the '''personal power level''' of both Story Character and [[Player Characters]].

The system '''blends''' <u>DnD-Styled</u> and  <u>Narrative Roleplaying</u> systems, finding a middle-ground between the ''complexity'' of the prior and ''freedom'' of the later. However, Firmata's Rolling System is '''narrative-first''', with rolls only functioning as a '''guide''' for <u>how to write a response</u>. There is '''no hard-coded health''' tracking. [[You]] decide how much your character can handle.

Featuring '''modularity''' allowing for both <u>character growth</u> and <u>equipment</u> opportunities, it is '''balanced''' for both world-native characters and multiverse arrivals entering [["The Continent"]].


== Rolling Mechanic ==
When performing rolls during Roleplay, send a message formatted like this without anything else:


The bot reads messages that start with a roll, and labels it with what you "rolled for"
 (roll) + (roll label)
 d20+7 Cinderpede Attack PHYS


'''You''' <u>write a response</u> for your '''attack''', then '''both you''' and the '''defender''' <u>roll</u>. Then the '''defender''' <u>writes a response</u> for their '''defense.'''

After, the '''Defender''' <u>writes a response</u> for their next '''attack'''. You '''both roll''', then '''you''' <u>write a response</u> for your '''defense'''.

Since this system is '''narrative first''', the roll results only determine <u>how a response will be written</u>.<br />Low rolls that may ''seem out of character'' can be equated to external factors like '''bad luck''', with the inverse being true for good rolls.

=== Combat Rolling ===
There are 2 typical scenarios you can find yourself in during combat: '''Melee''' or '''Ranged'''.

==== Melee Rolling ====
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
The '''Attacker''' rolls 💪[[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|Physicality]] to <u>launch their attack</u>.<br />The '''Defender''' rolls '''💪[[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|Physicality]]''' to <u>block</u> or <u>dodge</u> the attack.<br />'''Attacker''' →  '''Higher roll''' = The attack '''hits''', and the <u>difference</u> determines <u>damage severity</u>.<br />'''Defender''' →  '''Higher roll''' = The attack is either '''blocked''' or '''dodged''', with the <u>difference</u> determining <u>block</u> or <u>dodge effectiveness</u>.
</div>

==== Ranged Rolling ====
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
The '''Attacker''' rolls ⚡[[Firmata's Rolling System#⚡ Alacrity (ALC)|Alacrity]] to <u>aim their projectile</u>.<br />The D'''efender''' can either roll '''💪[[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|Physicality]]''' or ⚡[[Firmata's Rolling System#⚡ Alacrity (ALC)|Alacrity]] to <u>dodge</u> or <u>react</u> to the projectile.<br />'''Defender''' →  '''Higher roll''' = The projectile misses entirely and it's now '''the defender's turn''' to respond.<br />'''Attacker''' →  '''Higher roll''' = Both parties <u>roll again</u>.<br />This time, '''the Attacker''' rolls for 💪[[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|Physicality]] to <u>determine the projectile's strength</u> <small>(for '''gun users''', <u>only roll the gun's physicality</u>, character physicality '''does not''' affect gun strength)</small>.<br />Meanwhile, '''the defender''' rolls '''💪[[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|Physicality]]''' to <u>determine damage severity</u>.
</div>

== Core Stats ==
There are '''four fundamental stats''', each representing a trait in a character's capability.<br />💪[[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|Physicality]] | ⚡[[Firmata's Rolling System#⚡ Alacrity (ALC)|Alacrity]] | 💡[[Firmata's Rolling System#💡 Mentality (MENT)|Mentality]] | 🌙[[Firmata's Rolling System#🌙 Spirituality (SPIR)|Spirituality]]

----

=== 💪 Physicality (PHY) ===
<blockquote>''Raw bodily capability. strength, force, endurance, '''agility''', athleticism, pain resistance, and overall physical impact. <br />This determines what '''your body is capable of'''.''</blockquote>
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
* <small>Melee Aggression, Defense, and '''Speed''' <sup>(You need strong legs to run fast, after all)</sup></small>
* <small>Body-based feats</small>
* <small>Lifting, pushing, resisting</small>
* <small>Dodging physically</small>
* <small>Endurance tests</small>
</div>



=== ⚡ Alacrity (ALC) ===
<blockquote>''Reaction time, finesse, coordination, balance, accuracy, and movement precision.<br />This determines what you can '''make your body do'''.''</blockquote>
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
* <small>Aimed attacks</small>
* <small>Ranged accuracy</small>
* <small>Sleight of hand</small>
* <small>Precision dodging</small>
* <small>Quick reflex maneuvers</small>
* <small>Technique-based combat styles</small>
</div>



=== 💡 Mentality (MENT) ===
<blockquote>''Intellect, reasoning, focus, creativity, planning, memory, analysis, and rational decision making.<br />This determines '''what your brain can do'''.''</blockquote>
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
* <small>Strategy</small>
* <small>Investigation</small>
* <small>Solving problems</small>
* <small>Item/Equipment creation</small>
* <small>Complex techniques that require mental upkeep</small>
</div>



=== 🌙 Spirituality (SPIR) ===
<blockquote>''Soul intuition, emotions, wisdom, inner power, spiritual discipline, supernatural attunement, and magical/ritualistic practices.<br />This determines '''what your soul can do'''.''</blockquote>
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
* <small>Magic/[[Archkin]] abilities</small>
* <small>Manipulating [[The 4 Aspects of Reality]]</small>
* <small>Emotional resilience</small>
* <small>[[PSYCHE (Soul)|Soul]] pressure</small>
* <small>[[Faith]]-based or Spirit-attuned effects</small>
* <small>[[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Psychic]]/Empathic interactions</small>
* <small>Aura Projection</small>
* <small>Morality</small>
</div>


== {{Glimmer|full}} Resonance ==
'''Resonance''' determines how close a character has ''resonated with their fate'', how much they've '''achieved their''' '''full potential'''.<br />Typically, <u>younger</u> or <u>less experienced</u> characters get '''lower resonance''', indicating they still have '''lots of potential'''.<br />Meanwhile, <u>veteran</u> or <u>older</u> characters have '''higher resonance''', showing they've '''already peaked''' in potential.  

Resonance determines 2 key factors:  

# How many '''initial [[Firmata's Rolling System#Core Stats|stat points]]''' can be allocated <u>upon character creation</u>.
# How '''often [[Firmata's Rolling System#Resonance Attunement (Leveling Up)|new stat points]] may be granted''' by <u>Narrators</u> upon <u>character growth</u> moments.

=== Resonance Attunement (Leveling Up) ===
Characters can '''increase Resonance''' and gain more '''[[Firmata's Rolling System#Core Stats|stat points]]''' only through '''major narrative development''' — revelations, trauma, evolution, transformation, self-understanding, etc. These new points are given by [[Narrators]].

There are 2 kinds of new stat points:

# '''Growth Point''' → Reward for character development
# '''Resonance Realignment →''' When achieving a higher resonance tier, gain a bonus point depending on the achieved tier.

===== Resonance Realignment Example =====
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
<blockquote><small>Character goes from '''3{{Glimmer|full}} → 4{{Glimmer|full}} Resonance''':</small>

* <small>Starting Points for 3{{Glimmer|full}} = 3 points</small>
* <small>Starting Points for 4{{Glimmer|full}} = 5 points</small>
* '''<small>Difference = +2 points</small>'''
* <small>Plus '''+1 Growth Point'''</small>   
'''<small>Total: +3 points</small>'''

<small>But for '''2{{Glimmer|full}} → 3{{Glimmer|full}}''' '''Resonance''':</small>

* <small>Starting(2{{Glimmer|full}}) = 2 points</small>
* <small>Starting(3{{Glimmer|full}})= 3 points</small>
* <small>Difference = +1</small>
* <small>Plus Growth = +1</small>  
'''<small>Total: +2 points</small>'''
</blockquote>
</div>

=== Resonance Regression (Leveling Down) ===
On '''RARE CIRCUMSTANCES''' Characters do have a chance to '''lose resonance''' during certain character development moments, or if their [[memory]] gets wiped <small>(such as what happened in [[The Spirepocalypse]])</small>. If this were to ever happen, the only points lost are those granted from '''Resonance Realignment'''. Points gifted for generic character growth are '''permanent'''. 

Once <u>resonance returns to normal</u>, the reclaimed point/s may be '''reallocated''' to a <u>different [[Firmata's Rolling System#Core Stats|stat]] .</u>

== Equipment ==

=== Melee Tools ===
These items typically grant boosts to the [[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|💪 Physicality]] stat when used for their '''intended purpose'''. As this is for roleplay, '''the intended purpose can vary and be extended''' if <u>justified well enough</u>. A sword, intended for slashing, can still give its stat boost when cutting through leaves or webs.

=== Ranged Tools ===
For '''Bows''', '''Slings''', or any other ranged tool that '''requires the character's strength''', <u>character [[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|💪 Physicality]] is added by the tool's [[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|💪 Physicality]]</u>. <br />But for '''guns''' or any other ranged tool that '''doesn't need character strength''', you <u>'''only roll''' with the '''tool's [[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|💪 Physicality]]'''</u> . <small>(To balance this, guns typically have higher stats than bows.)</small>

=== Armor ===
Armor typically blocks [[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|💪 Physicality]] rolls against your character. However, optionally, '''damage types''' and '''armor coverage''' can be used as well.


===== Damage Types (Optional) =====
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
When enabled by both parties or a Narrator, damage types determine how well '''Slashing''', '''Piercing''', '''Blunt''', '''Heat''', or '''Psyche''' damage is mitigated by armor or caused by weapons. As this is a complication of the system, it is left to the Roleplayers to determine whether to use this or not.
</div>

===== Armor Coverage (Optional) =====
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
When enabled by both parties or a Narrator, <u>damage resistance</u> is '''only counted if an attack is directed at a body part that the armor covers'''. Even if a <u>helmet</u> gives +3 [[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|💪 Physicality]], if the attack is <u>aimed for the leg</u>, you <u>only roll your character's [[Firmata's Rolling System#💪 Physicality (PHY)|💪 Physicality]]</u> without the helmet's modifier.
</div>

== Hidden Stats ==
These stats '''cannot be assigned stat points'''. Instead, they can be acquired or diminished through <u>certain manners</u>.
----

=== 👁️ Lucidity (Luc) ===
<blockquote>''Level of [[Sentience]] and how greatly one can peer through The 4th's Wall.<br />This determines how much your character '''is aware of [[You]].'''''</blockquote>
<div class="mw-collapsible mw-collapsed">
* Resisting/Allowing [[You|your]] control
* Acting out of character
* Knowing things they shouldn't know
</div>

=== 🍀 Phantasy (LUCK) ===
<blockquote>''Blessings or curses from... something... tweaking fate to either favor or harm you.<br />This determines '''how fate favors you'''.''</blockquote>Unlike other stats, the Phantasy (Luck) stat '''affects all rolls'''.<br />If your character has +1 or -1 in this stat, you can either add or subtract a modifier to any roll you do.

As this stat is quite overpowered, it can <u>only be granted to characters</u> who have been '''blessed''' or '''cursed''', either from a '''god-like being''' in their original universes (for [["Otherworlders"]]) or through [[ᴷ³ᴿ³ᴷ¹ ᴮ²ᴬ⁴ᴹ⁷ᴿ²ᴬ⁰|ᴷ³ᴿ³ᴷ¹ ᴮ²ᴬ'''⁴ᴹ⁷ᴿ²ᴬ⁰''']]'''<nowiki/>'s influence'''.
