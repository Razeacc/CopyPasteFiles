It two-times reality with this 4th aspect to help form <u>Non-Existence</u>.<br />Together, all 4 aspects form the lands we walk on, and can't walk on.

<small>In the past, and partially in the present, they were '''manipulated''' by [[Archkin]] to perform <u>miracles</u> and <u>enact</u> their will.</small>

== Aspectual Applications ==
Think of these as the "'''type'''" of abilities  that Aspect Manipulation can do. While the aspects domain over certain parts of reality, applications are what the aspects do to these parts of reality.

These types aren't '''hard-set''', some abilities can be considered under <u>multiple applications</u> at once as there are many different formulas that can bring you to the same solution.<br />If you feel and ability fits better under a different application, your thought is valid and aren't wrong to think so. You're likely to reach the same effect regardless.

{{Material_Template|title1=Phantasy|image1=PhantasyAspect.png|type=Abstract and Nothingness|color=Cyan / Red}}
From the manifestations of your '''mind''' and the '''thoughts''' that race in your head, to the thinking process of one's '''creativity''' and the overwhelming dread of '''regret'''. 

{{AspectIcon|4}} '''[[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Phantasy]]''' is easily the '''most complicated aspect''' as it almost single-handedly governs the entirety of '''non-existence''' and '''abstract''' nature, only gaining assistance from {{AspectIcon|3}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Control|CONTROL]] in order to maintain the '''frequencies''' of [[PSYCHE (Soul)]] phenomena and [[Memories#Memory Amplitude|'''amplitude''']] of [[Memories]]. Outside of the vibrations from Souls, you can tell when <u>pure phantasmal phenomena</u> is occurring when you're in the presence of any '''2413Hz''' frequencies.

== Overview ==


Memory is a resource <u>embodied by anything and everything</u> that can '''think''' and '''choose''' for oneself. It is what's contained and utilized by ⁠[[Sentience]] in the formation of ⁠[[PSYCHE (Soul)|PSYCHE Energy]] ('''Soul''').<blockquote><small>Memory is governed by the [[Fracturing]] half of {{Phantasy}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Phantasy]].</small></blockquote>

== Memory Contents ==
Aside from the textbook definition of "Memories", it contains '''more''' than merely 'moments of time'.

Memories are '''innate'''. They contain '''Experiences''', '''Abilities''', and '''Natural Instincts'''. In <u>rare cases</u>, they can be '''passed down to offspring''', usually in a '''locked''' state. Offspring would need to unlock inherited memories to access them.<blockquote>{{#SQuote:|text=Knowing how to <u>breath</u>, how to <u>beat</u> your own <b>heart</b>, when to <u>blink</u>, <b>how</b> to blink, when you <u>think</u>—<br />how a bird <u>flies</u>, why your life <u>flashes before your eyes</u>, and how Archkin <u>manipulate</u> and <u>twist</u> their <b>lies</b>.|speaker=[[An Agent]]|receiver=|attribution=|source=|id=WLYA-NL6T-1A2J|template=}}</blockquote>Memories are the '''Instructions''' telling your soul what to do and how to do things. Whether or not you can actually process said instructions, and how well you process them, depends on your [[sentience]].

== Extracting Memories ==
Normally, you may simply '''ask someone''' holding certain memories to <u>retell</u> what they have to say.<br />Or, you could be '''bold''' and '''dastardly''', doing something '''not many others dare to do''' in the modern age:<br /><u>Read a book</u>.

However, if you mean how to '''extract''' memories, the process is a lot more hideous that you may expect.
[[File:Memory Piece Maker.png|thumb]]

=== [[Memory Pieces]] ===
The method to acquire ⁠[[Memory Pieces]] is quite the '''delicate''' procedure, as you need to extract both a <u>sliver of ⁠[[Sentience]]</u> while '''actively thinking of the memory''' or experience you wish to extract. This process is proven to be even more difficult for '''subconscious''' memories, such knowing how to <u>breath</u> or <u>beat</u> your own heart.

A ''certain apparatus'' is required, and it must be lodged into one's eye, the gateway to one's ⁠[[PSYCHE (Soul)|Soul]], to begin the process. More information can be found in the dedicate page to [[Memory Pieces]].

Extracting a memory does '''not''' always remove it from the host—though certain procedures, mishandled, are known to leave fragments or gaps. Bits of a memory may <u>stay and regrow</u>, and some extra memories <u>may be extracted as well</u>. The process isn't perfect when handled by '''mortals''', with a lot of stakes riding on mere random chance.

If [[PSYCHE (Soul)]] relies on unique frequencies to determine its color, Memories utilize '''Amplitude''' instead. == Overview ==
'''Sentience''' is a resource <u>embodied by anything and everything</u> that can '''think''' and '''choose''' for oneself. It is what contains and utilizes ⁠[[Memories]] in the formation of ⁠[[PSYCHE (Soul)|PSYCHE Energy]] ('''Soul''').

'''Quantity''' determines the <u>amount of memories</u> it can manage.<br />'''Quality''' dictates <u>how well</u> it can process and perceive [[memories]], playing closely to a Memory's [[Memories#Memory Amplitude|'''Amplitude''']].
Alongside its capability of processing memories, higher <u>categories of Sentience</u> slowly opens up one's '''[[Firmata's Rolling System#Hidden Stats|Lucidity]]''', allowing glimpses at the '''[[Beyonds]]'''. <blockquote><small>Sentience is governed by the [[NulGraven]] half of {{Phantasy}} [[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Phantasy]].</small></blockquote>

== Extracting Sentience ==
Sentience can be extracted through <u>various means</u>, as it can be found on '''any physical matter''' that is in constant contact with <u>living beings</u>.

When a '''physical part''' of a vessel's body is in <u>constant physical contact</u> with the vessel's '''sentience medium''':

* Nervous System
* Wires

Those parts become '''imbued with its sentience''', meaning it <u>contains Sentience:</u>

* Flesh
* Plating
* Blood
* Oils
* Fats
* Hair

In fact, most people often relate Sentience to '''DNA''', as it contains biological data from a living being. In the realm of ''Psyche Science'', '''<u>DNA</u> IS <u>Sentience</u>'''.

When those sentience-imbued parts leak out or disconnect from the vessel, it contains '''trace-amounts of sentience''' that lasts for a few minutes before slowly dissipating. This is how ⁠[[Aeleps]] and ⁠[[Ubir]] trigger their respective "'''blood'''" related traits, and how ⁠[[Marlimatas]] '''maintain''' their ⁠[[Khueenite]] bodies utilizing the trace sentience contained within <u>oils</u> and <u>fats</u>.

These are categories '''arranged in descending order''' based on how <u>aware</u> and <u>capable</u> each one is to process and utilize ⁠[[Memories]]. 

These categories are not hard set, and some vessels ''may possess traits from <u>different categories</u>''.<br /><small>Sentience is not a stat—it is a ''gradient.'' Vessels shift across categories depending on <u>cognitive strain</u>, <u>metaphysical exposure</u>, and <u>Lucidity</u>.</small>

== Overview ==
Due to the long-term connection of Soul to ⁠[[Archkin]], their '''frequency'''-resonating qualities have been '''imbued''' into the very concept of Psyche. For instance, the color/hue of your soul is determined by '''its natural frequency''', which varies from person to person, a trait directly linked to the frequencies of [[Archkin]].<br />In fact, it's often debated which came '''first''': The frequencies of <u>Archkin</u>, or the frequencies of <u>SOUL</u>.<br /><small>If you happen to know your soul's frequency, you may learn your soul's color by [https://www.flutopedia.com/sound_color.htm clicking here].</small>

== SOUL as an Energy ==
Soul is a truly powerful resource, some even refer to it as "'''The Zenith Energy'''", as it is the '''originator for all forms of energy.'''<br /><u>Heat</u>, <u>kinetic</u>, <u>sound</u>, <u>arcane</u>, <u>electrical</u>, <u>neurological</u>; some question how [[Archkin]] carry such great power within themselves.<br />But the true question is why such an invention was bestowed this level of privilege in the first place.

== Attuned and Unattuned Souls ==
[[File:Soul.gif|thumb]]
When harvested from certain sources and processed, Soul starts off as unattuned. This means the [[memories]] within it are all empty, and it does not vibrate at any frequency. It can still be used in this state, but its uses are <u>highly limited.</u>

The '''best use''' for Unattuned Souls is to <u>infuse it within a person</u>, attuning it to them and granting it power. Only when attuned can Soul do all manners of things to manipulate reality.<br />Only when attuned can it be considered "The Zenith Energy".

== Extracting Soul ==
The most common way of extracting Soul is through ⁠[[Memory Pieces]]. This is the '''easiest to transport''' and '''most reliable'''.

However, this method isn't as effective for '''mass operations''' or '''major projects'''. For a mass exploitation of Soul energy, one must rely on an ⁠[[Archkin]] capable of '''manipulating''' {{Phantasy}}'''[[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Phantasy]]'''.<br />It can also be done through '''machines''', but this technology is locked away amongst the most nefarious of individuals or organizations.

Extracting Soul is often seen as morally dubious and abhorrent, as it's essentially a '''slaughter''' of life. It is highly recommended against using this energy if you're wary of your own morality, or if you don't have any use for it anyways.<br />Either way, using it is difficult anyways considering most people lack the equipment for its utilization.

== Soul Flickers ==
[[File:Khueen SoulFlame.gif|left|thumb]]
These are a '''rare phenomena''' that aren't seen [[Beyond Eternity|very often]]. Unlike the light-like energy state Soul is typically found in, Soul Flickers feature a <u>plasma-like state</u>, taking on the appearance of a '''flame'''.

It is noted that these seem to carry a semblance of independent thought and sentience, acting like '''extensions of a being's soul''', like a <u>drone but for a soul</u>.
[[File:Beyond SoulFlame.gif|thumb]]
Not much is known about Soul Flickers due to their rarity. '''More information is needed''' for further analysis.

Some theorists believe Soul Flickers appear when a being's PSYCHE briefly '''overflows''' its vessel—whether due to <u>extreme emotion</u>, <u>fractured identity</u>, or exposure to <u>[[The 4 Aspects of Reality#Phantasy|Phantasmal]] phenomena</u>. None of these hypotheses have been confirmed.

They are harmless under <u>normal circumstances</u>, though prolonged contact is rumored to do... something.
