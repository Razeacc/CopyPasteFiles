# Overview
They're Scripted Fates' take on Succubi. Made by Morgale de Metso in An-Tri'Fhiana, archubi are humans that were infused with the properties of Fracturing Fissures.
Although being around them, or even a group of them, won't result in a Fracturing Zone, archubi are in a constant state of attempting to "superimpose" memories. This is a weakened version of fissures directly siphoning memories from those within its vicinity.

## Ability: Memory Superimposition
### How it works
While within the vicinity of an archubus, their innate Fracturing Fissure analyzes your soul's memories.
<small>(This process speeds up if the target has an exposed psyche, or within their Psyche Plane / Overwrite).</small>

Whenever the archubus does something that matches someone from your memories, they superimpose themself onto that other person in your mind, replacing them.
For instance:
>   You have a memory of how your mother held your hand at the park a few years ago.
>   You met an Archubus.
>   The archubus held your hand.
>   Nothing happens at first.
>   BUT, it just so happened the archubus started holding your hand just like how your mother did at the park a few years ago.
>   You start forgetting how your mother in that moment, and instead remember archubus there instead.
>   You begin to wonder if you've met this archubus before, despite this being your first meeting.

When they superimpose themself into someone's memory, that memory acts as a source of "sustinence" to them as it's slowly forgotten. Forgetting superimposed memories is how an archubi "digests" their nutrients.

### Memory Nutrients
The more superimposed memories an archubus has active at the time, the healthier and more lively they are. Each memory has different properties based on the memory holder's personality (psyche stats), with certain traits acting like nutrients such as protein or sugar.
However, the nutrient equivalent to each personality is still unknown. In fact, it's *uncertain whether the memory holder's personality or the memory's emotions itself are what constitues "nutrients"*.

### Range Limitations
Memory Superimposition relies on the target's soul "receiving" the "superimposing" frequencies from an archubus. Physical distance from an archubus is enough to avoid Memory Superimposition— as well as being behind a screen or radio signal. Just hearing/reading stories about an archubus is not enough to have memories superimposed as well.
This is because fracturing fissures cannot take memories away without your soul being physcially within their vicinity. Since archubi are already weaker fissures, this limitation is merely an extention of that mechanic.

However, once the memory has already been superimposed, *range is no longer a factor*. Memory Digestion sends the nutrients back to the archubus regardless of distance <small>(utilizing The Fracturing's quantum entanglement properties)</small>.

### New Memory Formation
Forming new memories with an archubi does nothing. Memory Superimposition, for the purpose of sustinence, relies entirely on the fact that the original memory STILL exists. The original memory with the original subject is merely blanketed by the superimposed memory with Archubi.

Two Archubi can theoretically superimpose each other in someone's memory, but there's a catch.
<u>Memory Superimposition requires the archubi to be more memorable than the person they're superimposing</u> in the memory. For most humans, this would be easy. But if it's a memory already superimposed by an archubi, this makes it increasingly more and more difficult to blanket that memory.

### Memory Measurement
Each "Memory" superimposed is a single moment in time, and each "moment" of memory has "nutritional mass". Theoretically speaking, an Archubi can only overwrite entire years of someone in a person's memory, if that someone has spent a total of several years-worth of memories within that person's memory. And even if, it won't be a constant single-take of memories. It's additive. And it all gets digested at the same time, over time.

### Memory Hunger Limit
An archubi can survive a total of 10 years without having any superimposed memories. This may seem a lot, but it's akin to a human being able to survive 10 years in total without food. If an Archubi goes for 3-5 days with no superimposed memories, that's 3-5 days total shredded off their seemingly-immortal lifespan.

## Archubi Reproduction
Archubi reproduction is somewhat controversial, as they don't follow typical reproductive cycles as humans— which Ubir, Aelep, and even Thingamakin technically follow. Instead, archubi need to find a human or alterant who's **already pregnant**.

From there, the archubus needs to somehow "convince" the mother that the archubus would be capable of raising the child or being a better parent than them. This process relies on the idea that the mother would've already daydreamed memories of their future with the child.
If the archubus successfully superimposes those daydreamed future memories of the mother and the child, with memories of the ARCHUBI and her child— this... uncomfortable method would result in the child being born as an archubi.
While they do still have the biological parent's genes, the newborn archubi would have the "Dominant Memes" of the archubi combined with the "Recessive Memes" of the biological parents.

### Memes over Genes
#### Dominant Memes
Scientifically, memes are the DNA of culture. Akin to memories, they're practices and methods passed-down from person to person.
For newborn archubi, this results in a majority of the parent archubi's habits and practices, being passed down to their child. All their social tricks, beauty practices, healthcare behaviors, and even occasionally some magical or archose-indused modi (if applicable).
However, there's stil the Biological Parents' recessive memes to take into account.

#### Recessive Memes
These are all the cultural ideologies and practices the parents actively refused, but remembered, in their life. This results in the archubi child having most of the archubi parent's traits, some of the biological parents' "personallly-negative" traits alongside their genes.

### Genetics vs Memetics
It is impossible to pass-down genetic material from an archubi. They are biologically sterile. Some may still have human reproductive organs, but not only are they usually inactive, their genetic material is incapable of being passed down due to their innate Fracturing Fissure biology having jammed the ability for their DNA to be inherited.
If forced, through whatever means necessary, the born child would most likely be riddled with severe birth defects due to "missing" pieces of DNA which are actually just unreadable for the child's body.

### Moral and Social Side-Effects
Due to how complicated and morally-divisive this process is, it has resulted in archubi being the least-populous alterant race across The Worlds Aplenty. For every thousands of ubir, there's believed to only be 1 archubus.
Their total population, prior to The Spirepocalypse, was estimated around 150-300 archubi.

## Ability: Innate Fracturing Connection
Due to the weird nature of their creation, all archubi share an ever-so subtle connection to The Fracturing and other archubi. This is how they were all able to at least "know of each other" prior to The Spirepocalypse. It's as if a force of gravity pulls fate towards each other. It also lets them innately sniff-out Fracturing Fissures through what almost feels like luck.

### Archose Caradelers
Their odd Fracturing nature makes it questionable how some archubi are able to use magic like other humans with archose in their soul.
In truth: Their Souls are capable of making archose caradeled. This key subtle-but-hidden feature was what Morgale's Living Piece capitalized on when making Caradeled Archkin in the first place.
This trait also makes any magic-like attack from an archubus subtly stronger against archkin, but weaker against caradels. It doesn't completely convert the archose's frequency into 2413Hz, hence why Morgale's Living Piece still needed more Caradel material to infuse when making Caradeled Archkin.

### Fracturing Repeater
This unique connection also makes them immune to the memory syphoning of Fracturing Fissures. Their presence even temporarily-extends the range of a Fracturing Zone, like someone pushing out a balloon from the inside.
While within zones, they also feel somewhat more satiated than normal as their fissures innately get fed from any memories taken away by the zone's fissures.

### NulGraven Ward
Around NulGraven Sites, they would actively target archubi first. This is due to their biology of being "psuedo Fracturing Fissures" making it so that their mere presence can slow down the spread of a NulGraven site.

### Decanoluzion Limitation
However, any Fracturing benefits stop if ever the archubus ends up in a Decanoluzion Zone. They cannot feed off these narrative-doomsday fracturing zones, nor can they extend it. They're just as much sentenced to-be annuled within these zones as much as everyone else.

# Culture
Due to how they're forced to live, evolution dictated only the most unique/memorable archubi survive and pass off their memes.

While they are often eye candy and heart stoppers, their memory superimposition causes lots of social problems. Some people are fine with this and willingly allows popular archubi to straight-up rewrite their memories. But others heavily-restrict and regulate them, with other nations even outright banning archubi completely.

Despite that, archubi manage to form somewhat of a tightly-knit circle amongst themselves, partially due to their low population. They don't mingle too much with non-archubi due to this, as such, they've formed certain unique memes exclusive to themselves.

## Feeding Habits
Due to how archubi memory superimposition works, it has formed psudeo factions of archubi who either follow "Pretty Bias" or "Negativity Bias" in their memology. This also resulted in a severe drought of "average/mundane" archubi.

### Pretty-Bias
Taking advantage of how humans often gravitate to attractive people, archubi who follow the "Pretty Bias" meme capitalize on this biological hardwire. They try their best to look good, feel good, and act good. With their diet consisting of mostly positive memories, they're quite controversial due to making people misremember key moments in their lives— such as weddings, first kisses, dates, hangouts, friendships, etc.
To these archubi, they feel like it's the safer option, as they get to give these people a "second moment" recreating these happy times... even if it does cost them the original memories.

### Negativity-Bias
Archubi who want the "easy way" of feeding themselves well, go down the path of "least resistance": fame through notoriety. Those that subscribe to the "Negativity Bias" meme actively harass, bully, annoy, or mildly-inconvenience people to quickly superimpose their bad memories of people with themselves.
These arcubi feel like they're doing the world a favor by making people forget who pissed them off or wronged them in the past. Instead of wanting revenge against a dude who kicked your knee, this archubus just kicked your knee again, now you misremember the past with the archubus being the one who kicked your knee all those years ago. 
Some call it martyrdom. Others just call them assholes.

## Current Status on The Continent (Firmata Frontier)
Some are living amongst The Stone Society unaware of their innate abilities, for now.
However, there are some currently asleep underground...

### The 10 Prisoners
Consisting of a mix between pretty-bias and negativity-bias archubi, these are several archubi that were left in "Psyche Petrification" within An-Tri'fhiana's prisons. Slated to serve "death sentences" for unknown reasons, they were technically the first victims of the Memory Wipe.
Before their execution, Morgale's Living Piece utilized their psyche-petrified states to harvest bits of their memory pieces to see if it could power herself up. It worked.
Those who saw this experiment sparked the initial rumors of The Memory Wipe before The Spirepocalypse.

# History
Originally, Morgale hoped for archubi to result in making humans that would be immune to a mass memory wipe, In theory, they would store their memories within their fissure bodies rather than a mind or soul. However, after several incompatibilities and being forced to utilize some of Lejos' unique caradel properties, the end result were the Archubi we know today.
Some believe their ability to "edit" memories is a mutation of how modular and adaptable Lejeseh caradel are.
