Idea: Similar to Skribbles, but without as much "customizability". Skravatars are empty husks that Fourth Marshals can freely control, pupetteer, and customize like a videogame avatar.

Firmata Frontier Discord Server, The Restricted Archives, Skravatar Workshop:
Immediately upon entering this room, a warning sign stands right before you.
Walking passed the sign, you're greeted by quite a similar sight to the Skribble Containment chambers.

While pale green flames flicker within jars, a massive blueprint also seems displayed right before you.
It's the manufacturing instructions for a Skravatar.

As a way for members of The 4th Beyond to directly interact with the Fictional World, Skravatars are entirely blank slates built for this exact purpose.

However, unlike Skribbles, Skravatars are entirely capable of customization. They can look however you want.
Even their personality is entirely dependent on you, acting as a direct holder for your will and consciousness.

Essentially, they are what some people call "Self-Inserts", albeit carrying much more ontologically-divine properties.

They are not recommended for battle, as a single direct hit can destroy a skravatar without a proper Fourth Marshal to perceive its damage.
It seems the main purpose of Skravatars is for Fourth Marshals to directly intervene and manipulate the fictional world without having to rely on following the story laid out by other skribbles, essentially turning you into a character in a story.
This is a way to "metagame" without breaking canon.

After further inspection, this workshop seems designed specifically for building Skravatars.
Although it has all the components and instructions, unless you're a Fourth Marshal, a juror cannot utilize the equipment in this room.

> Return to this channel occasionally and you may find a Blank Skravatar to collect.
> 
> NOTICE: Only a single skravatar is distributed at a time.
> Once another juror has collected the Skravatar, you must wait until another is available.
