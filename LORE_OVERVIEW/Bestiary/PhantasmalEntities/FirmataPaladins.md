## IDEA
Type: Phantasmal Constructs
Created by Lithe during his period of the Long Shush, they were originally 3 budding growths on Morgale's Living Piece's Failed Epicenter (god I should really just give her a proper name soon-).
By utilizing leftover Fracturing and NulGraven tech in the Lunore Conservation Site (Conservatory?), Lithe managed to develop these buds further into these giant crystalline guardians— the pinnacle of Avalon's Cariburn Archamachines: The Firmata Paladins.
They're unique constructs made from the unique circumstance of Morgale's Living Piece splitting herself between a Firmata (housing her mind) and a Failed Epicenter (the remains of her body).
Those buds were parts of the Living Piece's mind that were in the middle of escaping into HER Firmata before being caught up by the Failed Epicenter transformation.
