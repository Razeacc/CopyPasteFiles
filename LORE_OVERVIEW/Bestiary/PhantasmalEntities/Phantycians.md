## Idea
Nul Detta, Memetta, Dett Minor, Familiar Familiar, and Decanoluzioned Fictions. These are entities that act as "hiveminds" for properties that have "ceased to exist".

- Nul Detta - Souls
- Memetta - Teenagers
- Dett - Kids
- Familiar Familiar - Pets/Animals
- Decanoluzioned Fictions - Worlds 
- Simulette* - Alternate Realities
(Simulette's case is quite bizarre, as she exists almost entirely digitally and is not an actual phantycian).
