## Short Description
They are essentially no different from normal [[[[archkin]]]], aside from being newly-created.
This does open up several unique properties in them, with some being negative, and others positive.

## Fresh Soul
Due to how critical soul logic are with [[archkin]], alongside newly-created [[archkin]] not having enough experience with life in general, their souls are naturally easy to modify and access, similar to having the "Exposed Psyche" state.
While this "Freshness" degrades over time as they become more experienced, this leaves them vulnerable to creatues like Archubi, [[Caradeled [[archkin]]]], and other similar low-intensity soul-focused phenomena that normal [[archkin]] would be naturally resistant towards.

<small>Also, yes, the "Fresh Soul" is an archkin's equivalent of the soft spot atop a human baby's skull.</small.>

## Known Compatibilities
Here are some unique phenomena, or potential evolutionary states, applicable to Newborn [[archkin]].

### [[Caradeled archkin]]
While the soul is still vulnerable, a [[Caradeled archkin]] can overwrite certain memories in a Newborn [[archkin]] to turn them into another [[Caradeled archkin]].

### Fissuring [[archkin]]
This was essentially what Morgale de Metso truly is: They're Archkin capable of resonating at 2413Hz frequencies.
Either through direct witnessing/influence/manipulation by Nul Detta, or other similar methods, a Fissuring Archkin has essentially managed to break free from the weakness of 2413Hz seperatist frequencies.
In truth, there's no direct downside to this form of archkin, as if it's a direct evolution. It only becomes an issue with how difficult it is to become this form of archkin, alongside having to work with other archkin, as archose from Fissuring Archkin makes normal archkin noticably weaker due to its 2413Hz vibrations.

### A Fissuring Caradeled Exploit
A Surface-Level exploit can be seen in potentially making a __permanently-fissuring archkin__.
With the "Fresh Soul" property in Caradeled Archkin[^1] and the adventures of Archkin in Farewell Avalon, it's believed a caradeled archkin can simply hold a Memory Piece from Morgale and stay within range of "certain" Fracturing Fissures to stay Fissuring Forever.
Unfortunately, **Caradeled Archkin cannot turn into Fissuring Archkin**.
This is due to the properties of Fissuring Archkin relying on many base properties within normal Archkin, which includes Newborn Archkin. The process of becoming Caradeled Archkin removes and destroys several of these complex innate properties for the sake of "future-proofing". While that remains true, it means locking away the one upgrade possible with natural Archkin: Fissuring.
Not only that, it is currently unknown what Morgale exactly did to those certain fissures in Outpost 7 that allowed Archie to become fissuring while holding one of her memories around them. Much more study has to be done regarding those fracturing fissures.

## History
Following the arrival of the AoS at Avalon City, as they slowly worked on securing the city for their own use, The 4th Beyond inititiated their project of creating new [[archkin]] to repair and maintain [[The 4th's Wall]].

### Initial Creation
By taking the souls of people from The Worlds Aplenty before they pass over into The Afterlife, The 4th Beyond repurposes those souls into new [[archkin]].
<sub>(this unknowingly mirrors how Sandatta Ulhysse was made, without them even realizing it.)</sub>

To avoid an over-population problem with the now-increasing number of [[archkin]], all newborn [[archkin]] following The Creation War are given this innate will and purpose:
*   Gather more memories
*   Journey to [[The 4th's Wall]]
*   Find a hole in the wall
*   Fill in the hole with your body

### Farewell Avalon
[[Archie]] was the first test, the story of [[Farewell Avalon]] essentially being "how few memories can a new [[archkin]] even have upon creation?" This was why [[Archie]] started with no memories at this time.
All newborn [[archkin]] after them were made with varying stages of memory-reduction, some were made even further away as well (IE: Mesghal and Dustina in [[Lejos]], who were actually sent to HER Firmata).

While going through Outpost 7, they ran into a Memory Piece from [[Morgale de Metso]] prior to The AoS capturing her. Ever since they collected this, approaching certain fissures changed the archkin slightly. [[Archie]] temporarily turns into a fissuring archkin due to these fissures mistaking [[Archie]] as [[Morgale]]. Their Fresh Soul somewhat blurred who they really were, making the fissures accidentally see the not-fresh Memory Piece instead. It's akin to having a face perfectly fit for a mask, and carrying the mask by wearing it.

This allowed [[Archie]] access to travel between these Fracturing Fissures, while also gainin temporary immunities from 2413Hz within their vicinity.
At some point in Farewell Avalon, they find a device that lets them send a "relay message". It records a video message, confusing [[Archie]] at first, but [[Morga's Living Piece]] receives this message from Outpost 7 all the way in Outpost 3. During the time of Farewell Avalon, An-Tri'Fhiana was still active.

### A Relay
She, too, was confused by who the archkin could be, as [[Archie]] was never in The Creation War. That's when it hit her: The 4th Beyond was making new archkin.

Due to how skilled all archkin are at this point, it made it impossible to overwrite the memories of an archkin without an immense level of power. As The Living Piece was currently working on inventing Caradeled Archkin, she couldn't have the spread of Caradeled Archkin work on normal archkin due to its reliance on [[Arcubi]] memory replacement, it simply wasn't powerful enough.
However, by fine-tunning the Caradeled Archkin's Caradel Spread rewriting to ONLY work on these newborn archkin, not only would it make the process faster and more efficient, it could also help actually make this new breed of archkin achieve one of its purposes: to evolve the archkin race passed its weakness from 2413Hz into something capable of defeating Sandatta Ulhysse (or other beings similar to her).
