# OVERVIEW
A metaphysical energy made from brain activity, it is a Fourth Beyonder's equivalent to a Skribble's scripting ink, or a Wordbuilder's Raw Creativity. Imaginary Flow is how creatives can perform creative acts in general. It's almost impossible to measure, as the recovery rate, maximum capacity, and usage rate varies wildly from individual to individual, and action to action. It's used to control skribbles, use wordbuilders, and create art.
