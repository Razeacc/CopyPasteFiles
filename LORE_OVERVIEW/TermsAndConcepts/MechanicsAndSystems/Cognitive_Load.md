# Overview
Utilized mostly in Epicenters/Concepts, "Cognitive Load" is an abstract cost/resource that functions similarly to Bandwidth or RAM in systems. Although it's near-impossible to measure exact consistent scales and values (partially to make writing easier, and to give it a stronger sense of mystique), it's meant to be simple enough to slide into any complex mechanic to help "dumb it down" partially.

Most creatures have irrelevant maximum cognitive loads. But in order to use certain abilities, some cognitive load needs to be allocated. This essentially acts as space occupying brain processing capabilities.
The more cognitive load in-use, the slower a creature's thinking process becomes— both instinctual and active thinking <small>(although, a heavier emphasis and load is weighed onto active thought over instincts and subconscious)</small>.

## Cognitive Overload
When overloaded, the user may begin forgetting details immediately, get overwhelmed emotionally/mentally/spiritually, and hallucinate details akin to LLMs.
Any standard "mana-like" internal resource gets expended faster alongside showing signs of "low mana" commonly-seen in  media, and recovery times of all mental variables (mental fatigue, mental debuffs, etc.) slow down as well based on how overloaded one's cognition is.

## Determining Maximum Cognition
Based on the user's Alacrity for the Physical Cognition, averaging out with a combination of their Mentality (physical mind) and Spirituality (metaphysical mind) determines the "quality" and "quantity" of one's Cognitive Load. Other factors, such as Epicenterhood (as some Epicenters can actually help shoulder the Cognitive Burden) and Boons (IE: Connection to Nul Detta, intense Cognition Training, etc.) can also help increased one's Cognition.

### Cognition Training
Done by overwhelming the senses and intentionally entering an overloaded state, this actively breaks down the maximum physical cognitive load one may access. After physically recovering, this load is hardened, granting better cognitive quality.

Increasing actualy Cognitive Quantity is more difficult, as it requires injecting archose, or any other magical subsance, into one's soul to help act as batteries or capacitors for one's metaphysical mind to carry more cognitive load. It's akin to using the archose of an archkin to process cognition for you.


### Cognitive Quality
Better quality determines how quickly the mind can adapt to and process reductions in Cognitive Load, and how how slowly it increases received load. Cognitive Load changes aren't instant. Better quality can help the user adjust to increases more gradually, helping them determine how much they can handle before overloading.
Poor quality may even cause cognitive load to not only spike up, but also rebound and "wobble", artifically increasing load temporarily during spikes.

### Cognitive Quantity
Determines purely how much cognitive load one can handle before overloading.
It's a LOT simpler than Cognitive Quality.
