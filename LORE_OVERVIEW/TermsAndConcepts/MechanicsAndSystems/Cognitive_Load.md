# Overview
Often called "*Psyche Strain*" or "*Mana Capacity*" in more fantastical settings/context, **Cognitive Load** is the overarching mental and spiritual "resource" system used in the Scripted Fates series.
Its usage spans widely, from the most overwhelming Epicenters and Mana-slingers in the verse, to mere office workers multitasking paperwork. Just like how kilograms measure mass, calcius measures temperature, and watts measures electricity— "Load", "Strain", "Burden", "Capacity/Reserves" all measure how much the brain/mind/soul is processing at once.
*   Mages say:
> My mana reserves are low.

*   Scientists say:
> My cognitive burden is nearing overload.

*   Vicars say:
> My psyche is strained.

*   Computers say:
> `FATAL ERROR: Ineffective mark-compacts near heap limit Allocation failed - JavaScript heap out of memory`

They're all describing the same phenomenon: **Cognitive Load**.
> <small> There is a subtle difference.
> Cognitive Load is often used for mental/logical/scientific tasks, while Psyche Burden is for spiritual/magical/fantastical tasks. The terms can be used interchangably based on the writer, but this is the standard procedure.</small>

It is an abstract resource that functions similarly to Bandwidth or RAM. It is technically possible to attach a value to it, but that number will never be 100% accurate.
> <small>(This allows for measurements to change and adjust from project to project to better-fit game design... and to make writing easier while adding mystique).</small>
Although recording consistently is near-impossible, it's meant to be simple enough for almost any setting/mechanic. It may even be added to a complex mechanic and help "dumb it down".

All creatures with SOUL are subjected to Cognitive Load. It essentially acts as space occupying brain processing capabilities. Normally, it's almost unnoticeable. But when performing complex spells or actions, that's when a noticeable amount of cognitive load needs to be allocated.

## Cognitive Overload
The more cognitive load in-use, the slower a creature's thinking process becomes— both instinctual and active thinking.
> <small>(although, a heavier emphasis and load is weighed onto active thought over instincts and subconscious)</small>.
When overloaded, the user may begin forgetting details immediately, get overwhelmed emotionally/mentally/spiritually, and hallucinate details akin to LLMs.

Based on how overloaded one's cognition is, any standard "mana-like" internal resource gets expended faster alongside showing signs of "low mana" commonly-seen in  media. Recovery times of all mental variables (mental fatigue, mental debuffs, etc.) are also slown-down.
The easiest way to experience Cognitive Overload in real life is by overstimulating yourself. From high-paced videos, crowded social gatherings, or even simply loud noises.
> <small>(another reason explaining why sounds/vibrations/frequencies == magic)
The overwhelming lightheaded feeling you get from overindulgence in stimuli is exactly what happens during Cognitive Overload. In fact, some may argue it literally is cognitive overload.

## Determining Maximum Cognition
Based on the user's Alacrity for the Physical Cognition, averaging out with a combination of their Mentality (physical mind) and Spirituality (metaphysical mind) determines the "quality" and "quantity" of one's Cognitive Load. Other factors, such as Epicenterhood (as some Epicenters can actually help shoulder the Cognitive Burden) and Boons (IE: Connection to Nul Detta, intense Cognition Training, etc.) can also help increased one's Cognition.

### Cognition Training
Done by overwhelming the senses and intentionally entering an overloaded state, this actively breaks down the maximum physical cognitive load one may access. After physically recovering, this load is hardened, granting better cognitive quality.

Increasing actual Cognitive Quantity is more difficult, as it requires injecting archose, or any other magical substance, into one's soul to help act as batteries or capacitors for one's metaphysical mind to carry more cognitive load. It's akin to using the archose of an archkin to process cognition for you.

### Cognitive Quality
"RAM Speed". Better quality determines how quickly the mind can adapt to and process reductions in Cognitive Load, and how how slowly it increases received load. Cognitive Load changes aren't instant. Better quality can help the user adjust to increases more gradually, helping them determine how much they can handle before overloading.
Poor quality may even cause cognitive load to not only spike up, but also rebound and "wobble", artificially increasing load temporarily during spikes.
> Training Recommendation: Watch Max0r Videos on loop

### Cognitive Quantity
"RAM Size". Determines purely how much cognitive load one can handle before overloading.
It's a LOT simpler than Cognitive Quality. In fact, this is just Maximum Cognition, why did I make a second header for this-
Other way to increase Cognitive Quantity, that don't involve archose harvesting or Nul Detta bargaining, is to eat foods that promote brain health and memory management.
> Training Recommendation: Omega-3 vitamins are the way to go for those without access to an Archose Incubator.

## Archose Training
If you do happen to have archose within your soul, performing training with your archose (either through recollecting and bridging the gap between your memories and the archkin's memories in the archose, or by having more FAITH in yourself) is another way to increase BOTH Cognitive Quality AND Quantity. However, this option isn't avaliable to most.

# Compatibilities

## Spell System
Longer and more complicated spells take more Cognitive Load (Psyche Burden) to cast/use. This is quite literally due to how complex and long the commands for these spells can be. However, the cost is more obvious with innate magic users due to the Cognitive Burden being carried by their the internal archose/mana/magic in their souls.
Spell Cartridge users, however, merely deal with the Cognitive Load of casting the spell itself. However, since they don't have innate archose, the burden actually evens out to be roughly the same.

## G-Forces
While High-G movement feels like increasing Cognitive Load, in truth, it actually decreases your maximum load while under its effect due to the loss of blood to the brain.
Of course, creatures that don't use blood can ignore this subheader.
