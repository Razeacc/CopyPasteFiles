# Idea
## Overview
Acting as multiversal read/write Singletons across all of fiction, Epicenters are the pinnacle backbones of ideas and phenomenon across The Worlds Aplenty.
Although most Epicenters are unbound and holder-less, the few that do have holders grant them unparalleled access and privilege over all occurences of the assigned idea, at the cost of immense perceptual strain.
Epicenterhood subjects yourself to constantly "Reading" every single instance of the idea you're an Epicenter of. Whether or not you can actually "Write" into those instances, depends on how well you can even handle the strain of having the epicenter in the first place.

Epicenters are the gravity awells of ideas and phenomena, allowing them to flow from the abstract to reality.

## Epicenters vs Concepts
Furthermore, you may often see Epicenters referred to as "Concepts". **Concepts are no different from Epicenters**, it's merely a more *poetic* secondary terminology to contrast "Epicenter"'s scientific / literal definition.

# How Epicenters are made

## Witnessing
The most common, and easiest method, for achieving Epicenterhood. By being witnessed by The 4th Beyond, one can achieve Epicenterhood, or maybe even modify certain states of their epicenter. This is often done through a voting process, to either Witness or Actively Deny a candidate.

## Drive
Rather than relying on being Witnessed, some Epicenters develop such a ferverous drive over an idea, that they "force the hand" of the story itself to make them the epicenter of something. This is often used for Homebrewed or Wuthering Epicenters, due to how volatile and "unorthodox" this method is.

# Alteration States
Epicenters occasionally have different states/statuses that modify them from the standard.

## Divided
Attempts to achive Shared Epicenterhood that ends in failure. Divided Epicenters are actually weakened, as it often locks out certain instances of an idea out of both Write AND Read priviledges from all Epicenter Holders with this state. 

## Shared
Able to lighten to cognitivie load, this rare Epicenter class is only applicable to Epicenters whos idea would actually be compatible with this state. If not, it defaults to Divided.
Shared Epicenters act akin to a Google Docs file. Those with this Epicenter have read access to all instances of this idea. They can also write/edit them live at once.
This Epicenter can be customized and reorganized if necessary, such as modifying it to function more like a Repository, where all other Epicenter Holders need to approve changes before edits are made, but all can Read at once.

## Wuthering
Temporarily Short-lived Epicenters, usually relying on a pre-existing Epicenter to syphon "Epicenter Priviledge" from.

## Heretical
Almost identical to normal Epicenters, although it's often the case where a Heretical Epicenter and a normal Epicenter of one trait cannot exist at the same time. There are ways to loophole around this, such as creating a Firmata where only your version of the heretical concept is active. Or, some Heretical Epicenters are capable of overwriting the main Epicenters entirely, but only temporarily. In this method, Heretical Epicenters become akin to Wuthering Epicenters.

## Synolutioned
It's typically not stated nor added to the Epicenter's name. Synolution is the process of an Epicenter grasping an even finer and more-specific idea extended from itself, using the base/initial idea.
Taken from the words "Synergize" and "Evolution", the phenomena happens upon major spurts of character development. Not all Epicenters experience Synolution.
Synolutioned Epicenters are a direct upgrade, at the cost of a higher cognitive load, as you become the holder of, essentially, two epicenters.

## Metaphor
This alteration is similar to "Concept" and "Epicenter", where it replaces the actual adjective of the title.
However, unlike the two meaning the same thing (just with the former being the more poetic name and later being its scientific name), being a Metaphor entitles oneself to absolutely zero benefits or downsides. They merely embody the idea they "Metaphorize" to the absolute best of their abilities. They cannot perceive all instances of that idea akin to a singleton, similar to Epicenters and Concepts.
Instead, it seems something greater awaits Metaphors as fate continues onward.
Sometimes, a Metaphor may become a proper Epicenter, or even something greater.

## Boundless / Unbound
Merely the term used for Epicenters that have no holder.
You will never see someone with a Boundless Epicenter/Concept... under normal circumstances.

## Homebrewed
Usually the outcome of Epicenters made from one's will, these are bizarre epicenters that often act as singletons for proper nouns or specific ideas (IE: people). 

---

On an additional side note: Carriers of Epicenters are called "Holders" by default, but the term can change to match the Epicenter better.
Such as the Concept of Memories' holder being a "Recollector", or the Epicenter of Simulation being a "Singleton".

# Compatibilities

## Psyche Planes
Psyche Planes of Epicenters are often the most bizarre and expressive ones, as they fully reflect the idea they embody. Within Epicentral Psyche Planes, it's more oftne than not, that the entire environment of the plane itself IS the spiritual body of the holder.
The only tim where this won't be the case, is the final battle in Firmata Frontier season 1 against Wuthering Concept of Self-Destruction Besado and Epicenter of Sentience / Sacrifice Memetta. But this is due to their psyche planes essentially crashing into each other, alongside roleplayers entering Besado's already crashed-into Psyche Plane. On top of that, Besado was a Wuthering Epicenter, meaning the odds were stacked against them for Besado having an actual body within her plane, and not her body being the plane itself.
This same logic is also applied to Overwrites.
