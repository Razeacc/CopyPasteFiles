# Overview
By directly pulling someone into your Psyche Plane, you enter a Psyche Duel— a fight within one's own soul.
While within a Psyche Duel, time does not pass outside, nor does pain transfer over from the Psyche Plane into the Physical realm (unless it's stated that the only way to damage an entity is through attacking them within their Psyche Plane). These duels are perfect for training, symbolic fights, and showcases of skill.
If defeated within a Psyche Duel, the defeated individual will leave the Psyche Plane exhausted, with their ability to use any psyche/magical capabilities reduced slightly.
