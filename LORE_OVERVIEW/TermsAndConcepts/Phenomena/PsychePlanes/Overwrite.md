# Overview
It is essentially a Psyche User's own Fracturing Zone or NulGraven Site: a physical space that's an extension of one's psyche into the physical realm. For amateurs, it merely paints or "overwrites" the world. But for more experienced users, it can potentially even add or superimpose itself onto the terrain to create new temporary structures.

## Differences between Psyche Duels and Overwrites
* **Timeflow**: While Psyche Duels happened within the plane itself, Overwrites pulls the Psyche Plane into reality— timeflow is uninterrupted, unless specified.
* **Weakening**: Overwrites are generally weaker than Psyche Duels, as the added real-world materialization expends some focus that would've been used in a more potent Psyche Plane.
* **Consequences**: During an Overwrite, pysical damage is retained, unlike Psyche Duels where it's transfered into Psyche Damage or nullified entirely at the end of the duel. Furthermore, in a Psyche Duel, losing causes your ability to use Psyche Skills to weaken greatly. In contrast, it is instead the inscriber of an Overwrite that gets their Psyche Skills weakened following an Overwrite.
* **Instability**: Ovewrites are highly unstable comprared to planes and duels, as Overwrites are subject to Intrusive Bleed.

However, just like Psyche Planes, not all Overwrites are combat-focused, as some Overwrites can instead be used to help find clues, reveal footprints, display "interactables" (commonly used by videogame character-controlled skribbles), create temporary resting points, etc.

# Factors

## Witness Clause
Those with a soul capable of witnessing (hear, feel, see, sense, etc.) the incantation, motion, or mere intent to inscribe an Overwrite, will then __witness the user's soul stretch out from their eyes/soul__ and into the world around them, they get dragged into the Overwrite (this does not include outside artificial distance). Overwrites do not target enemies, they target  ovservers. It takes immense skill for an overwrite to specifically target individuals rather than everyone.
Furthermore, anyone entering the vicinity of the Overwrite after it was cast, would also be dragged in. This continues until the Overwrite is cancelled.

As massive flowing flows of ink decorating the canvas of reality, a psyche plane has now overwritten the physical world.

### Artificial Distance
The only thing that can separate you from being dragged into an Overwrite. As long as your soul isn't the one witnessing the Overwrite, you're safe from being pulled into it.
**Protected Artificial Distances**
* Phone Call
* Video Stream
* Camera Footage
**Unsafe "Spiritual" Distances**
* Telepathic Link
* Shared Dream
* Direct Soul Projection

## Intrusive Bleed
The longer an Overwrite is maintained, the more instrusive thoughts and deep secrets leak out into the surface, potentially hindering the inscriber of the Overwrite. This is directly the consequence of an unstable and wavering psyche plane and a distorted sense of self.

Speed of Instrusion Bleed setting in is determined by Intrustion Rate.
Meanwhile, Instrusion Severity determines how violent the Intrusive Bleed can be.
Psyche Plane Mastery can offset Intrusion Bleed slightly, but it will always have a disadvantage due to the innate biological negativity bias of most sentient creatures.

As Intrusive Bleed settles in, some details can occur, which are generally consistent between almost all Overwrites.
*   **Early bleed** causes slight distortion in distant environmentals and sublte whispers in the air.
*   **Moderate bleed** starts manifesting regretful memories and blury figures from the past.
*   **Severe bleed** has the Overwrite begin arguing or rejecting commands from the inscriber.
*   **Critical bleed** makes the Overwrite borderline sentient as it begins lashing out on everyone within in an act of self-hatred and self-destruction. This eventually causes the Overwrite to destroy itself prematurely ahead of the inscriber's intention.    

### Intrustion Rate
Weaker psyche/spirituality raises Intrustion Rate.

### Intrustion Severity
Regrets, doubts, worries, fears, and other similar negative emotions raise Intrustion Severity.


## Multiple Overwrites
When several inscribers inscribe their Overwrite, the hierarchy is determined by the Overwrite's current Instrusive Bleed. This is the main factor essentially dictating the "overall health" of an Overwrite. The lower one's intrusive bleed is, the higher hierarchy it has. This means even "weaker Overwrites" can potentially overpower stronger ones, if the inscriber of the weaker plane has considerably more confidence and less self-doubt.

The hierarchy levels/layers come in 4 categories:
### Base Layer
The main and strongest Overwrite currently in-effect. All other Overwrites must adhere to the base layer's logic and effects, or suffer dampening consequences.
All other "weaker" layers are categorized based on how the inscriber feels about the Base Layer, there is no layering hierarchy. Only the Base Layer, and everything else below it.

#### Annotations
Meant for inscribers who have **positive feelings** to the Base Layer Overwrite, they're able to add onto the base layer through additional new rules or factors to take into account.

#### Revisions
For those with more **negative outlooks** on the Base Layer, this Overwrite dampens and changes certain factors in the Base Layer to a degree based on how incompatible it is. The more incompatible, the more drastic the revisions.

#### Marginalia
An outlier in the categories, Marginalia Overwrites are for those **wishing to prematurely escape** the Overwrite. Their Overwrite would attempt to carve a path through the Base Layer to help the Inscriber leave, or at the very least, survive within the Base Layer.
It can inscribe a miniature Overwrite around the inscriber, keeping it small and compressed to act as a safety barrier around the inscriber. If the Base Layer is weak enough, or the inscriber is strong enough, a path would be carbed out instead.

While Psyche Duels are still possible, its limits and restructions have been broken in this new replacement for it, something that now stains The Era of The Spirepocalypse:
Overwrite.

# How to inscribe an Overwrite
First, a user needs to be **aware of Overwrites** in the first place, awareness is key, similar to how awareness of spell incantations shortcuts is important to actually use spell shortcuts. Overwrite is, in of itself, a type of "global spell", after all.
Second, depending on which "Frontier Stat" you prioritize (or which one you personally prioritize), **make a certain gesture** (custom gestures being possible if you can't make any of these motions, it just needs to be a "pose that reflects yourself the most").
*   **PHYSICALITY**: Press your fists together or "reload" your fists (punching them outwards)
*   **ALACRITY**: Close or squint your eyes
*   **MENTALITY**: Press a finger to your temple or press a fist to your chin in a "thinking" motion
*   **SPIRITUALITY**: Grasp your heart, or pump your chest
Third, **shout "Overwrite"**, which acts similarly to a spell. It commands the archose within their soul to inscribe the psyche pane outside of their body and into the world, overwriting the world around them.
