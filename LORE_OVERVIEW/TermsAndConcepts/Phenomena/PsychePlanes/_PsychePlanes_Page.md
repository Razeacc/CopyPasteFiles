# Overview
A mirror into one's soul, reflecting who they truly are in a visualized environment.
Psyche Planes are pocket dimensions within one's soul, which can be utilized in various manners, from combat to experimentation. Although it's mostly utilized by archkin, this is merely due to how they're quite literally "living soul batteries". Anyone, with a strong enough soul (and perhaps some archose in their soul), is capable of manifesting a Psyche Plane.
Every soul possesses a psyche plane, though not everyone is capable of consciously entering or manipulating it. Psyche Planes are not inherently battlegrounds. Rather, they are symbolic reflections of identity, memory, values, fears, aspirations, and personal truth.

## Mastery
Determined by one's psyche abilities and their personal self-confidence and faith in oneself, Mastery is how refined and clear a Psyche Plane is to its user. It showcases how accurate the plane is to who the user is, how clear and well-defined it can be (with lower mastery resulting in blurry or inconsistent planes), and how much it can manifest actual structures as Overwrites, rather than merely overlaying the environment.
Mastery ranges from:
* Perfect
* Skilled
* Accustomed
* Adjusting
* Blurry

## Psyche Plane Samples
### Kalayan's Nameless Arena
* **Type**: Combat
* **Mastery**: Accustomed
* **Aspect**: Space
> A boxing arena where all magical abilities are nullified and Kalayan's physical prowess matches the average physicality of the others in the plane.
> However, secretly, Kalayan is still using his shapeshifting to rapidly shrink his body when "getting hit" to avoid any actual damage. One needs to realize this trick and take him down through a grapple instead— which is tricky considering he can't be hurt through other means.

### Morgale's Endless Experiment
* **Type**: Utility
* **Mastery**: Skilled
* **Aspect**: Space
> An endless white void filled with massive grey cubes connected via bridges, the scene looks awfully similar to how neurons and synapses appear in a brain. This is where Morgale has complete administrative control where she normally spends her time within to conduct experiments through the simulation of her Psyche Plane befire bringing them into reality.

### Khueen's Mirror Mirror
* **Type**: N/A
* **Mastery**: Beyond Perfect
* **Aspect**: Phantasy
> A mirror world that reflects back the deepest and darkest regions of a target's psyche plane. Its type depends entirely on what the target's psyche plane would be. And, no matter how skilled someone is at using their plane, or how much they've self-discovered to hide their own flaws and weaknesses— Khueen will always be more skilled at using your own psyche plane against you, alongside knowing what and where your absolutely darkest memories reside. This, unintentionally, makes the plane perfect for psycho-therapy.
> The only way to actually see Khueen's Psyche Plane, is if she lets you. There's no way to bypass the "reflect your own soul back at you" mechanic, as Khueen directly backdoors the very concept of Psyche to pull off this feat. No matter how much resistance you have to psyche effects, and now matter how much you can fight back— Khueen will always have the upper hand, it's been inscribed directly into the concept of psyche.

### Vektat's Firmata Showdown
* **Type**: Utility
* **Mastery**: Perfect
* **Aspect**: Space
> As Vektat's Firmatas aren't actual Overwrites, they don't function like them, nor are they subject to rules like Intrusive Bleed. Rather, when Vektat pulls you into his Psyche Plane, he creates endless "bubbles" that copy the time-frozen contents within each of his Firmatas. Pushing you to the edge of one bubble sends you to another Firmata bubble. Vektat has full control over which firmata you get sent to.


# Psyche Plane Applications
From the phenomena of Psyche Planes, two application branches were eventually formed.
The traditional process of entering one's Psyche Plane for a low-stakes symbolic fight: **Psyche Duels**.
And recently, following The Spirepocalypse, the phenomena of leaking one's Psyche Plane into reality: **Overwrites**.
