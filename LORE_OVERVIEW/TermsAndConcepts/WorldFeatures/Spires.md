# Overview
Massive beams and pillars of spirite shooting out from the ground (similar to a Minecraft Beacon), Spire act as The Worlds Aplenty's equivalent to volcanoes. They serve as the main drivers of Exspanse formation, mineral generation/convertion, and also function as an... almost... reliable source of fuel and energy.
All Spires are ever-so subtly tilted/angled slightly to the east, as all Spires eventually bridge to the top of The 4th's Wall while passing through the Timeline Sky.

## Types of Spirite
*   **Cyan** spirite vibrates at 2413Hz. It is hazardeous to archkin and Fourth-aligned entities such as scribbles. Those with Beyonder protection are able to be relatively unharmed by it when travelling up spires of this substance.
*   **Pale Green** spirite vibrates at 3142Hz. It is hazardeous to archkin and Nul-aligned entities such as caradels. Those with Beyonder protection are still able to be relatively unharmed by it when travelling up spires of this substance.
*   **Gold** the original spirite created by Sandatta Ulhysse, uncorrupted by The Null and unpolluted by The AoS, they're safe for archkin to travel up and down from. However, due to Vicars and the AoS, these gold spires are becoming increasingly rare.

## Spirite Leylines

Acting as this world's equivalent of "lava veins/channels" while also combining the fantasy concept of "leylines" they push spirite all across The Infinite Fields in rapid speeds. They slowly transmutate and manipulate the stone around it, shifting them into minerals that slowly get pushed higher or lower based on density.
