## Overview
> A **Firmata** (plural: *Firmatas*) is an artificially contained reality designed to simulate stable civilizations, ecosystems, and historical continuity. Originally conceptualized by Kaitzar and the Unnamed Fourth Marshal, Firmatas are deployed on a massive scale by the **Arch of Sentients (AoS)** to contain and isolate entire civilizations from each other. 
> 
> To ensure global populations remain isolated, contained, and completely oblivious to the outside world, the AoS struck a massive geopolitical compromise with **Vektat, the current Archgod Dragon**. Tired of the AoS’s endless, exhausting attempts to capture or kill him, the godlike dragon agreed to terms of peace. In exchange for being left entirely alone, Vektat manufactures the colossal, perfectly synchronized Firmatas that encapsulate entire Exspanses, including Lejos, Locunex, Movakoz, and many others. 

---

## Firmata Cores
Every Firmata operates as an isolated bubble universe governed by a single, critical structural rule: **it requires a Core**.

A Firmata Core acts as a programmatic singleton and a localized "mini Epicenter" that anchors the reality, ties its systems together, and dictates its temporal rules.

## Time Flow
Controlling the flow of time within a Firmata requires immense skill, but the quality of the Core determines its ultimate stability:

*   **Static State (Default)**: The baseline for basic Firmatas, where internal time is entirely frozen relative to the outside world.
*   **Time Dilation (Advanced)**: Forcing internal time to exponentially accelerate (faster) or decelerate (slower) compared to external reality.
*   **Temporal Looping (Expert)**: Locking a contained reality into an infinite, repeating time loop.
*   **Perfect Synchronization (Mastery)**: The absolute pinnacle of Firmata engineering, matching the internal timeline perfectly with the passage of external time. Because Vektat is the second strongest Archgod currently in existence, his reality-bending spatial manipulation makes this effortless. He merely sheds a single dragon scale to act as the Core. This scale generates massive, flawlessly synchronized, and unshakeable Exspanse-sized cages.

## Firmata Scale
The structural size of a Firmata is also a showcase of craftsmanship. A geographically-massive but temporally-static Firmata can be far more difficult to construct than a smaller, time-dilated one. Whichever feat requires more skill, sheer spatial volume or complex temporal anomalies, is currently unknown, as the extremely limited number of active "Firmata smiths" results in highly unreliable in-universe testimonies.

## Known Firmatas

### THEIR Firmata (The Outer Layer)
*   **Creator**: Vektat, the Archgod Dragon (provided to the AoS).
*   **Firmata Core**: A single, discarded scale of Vektat.
*   **Status**: Active
*   **Size**: Exspanse
*   **Time**: Synced
*   **Description**: The colossal, overarching Firmata encapsulating the entire Lejos Exspanse. Vektat willingly volunteered to cage Lejos out of a deep, ancient grudge: he loathes the people of Lejos for doing absolutely nothing to investigate the tragic death of the First Archgod Dragon long ago, fiercely believing their public mourning did nothing but bury his fallen comrade deeper into oblivion.
*   **AoS Social Engineering**: To keep the 1940s–1980s retro-tech society from ever discovering the sky is an artificial cage, the AoS actively infiltrates their civilization. Operating through a powerful, secret puppet corporation, the AoS sneakily intercepts media and socially manipulates the populace. They intentionally manufacture ideological gridlocks and civil-war-esque social conflicts (such as weaponizing the "Domers" slur) to keep the Lejeseh too busy fighting each other to ever look up and see the grand lie.
*   **Note**: This specific blueprint and structural setup is replicated identically for all other Firmatas manufactured by Vektat across the world.

### HER Firmata (The Inner Dome)
*   **Creator**: The Living Piece of Morgale de Metso.
*   **Firmata Core**: The Failed Epicenter of Morgale.
*   **Status**: Active
*   **Size**: Continental
*   **Time**: Severely-desynced Acceleration
*   **Description**: A localized, highly volatile Firmata manifested directly into the central wilds of Lejos to trigger the Spirepocalypse. Manifested without Vektat's flawless spatial mastery, and created while Morgale was simultaneously siphoning countless memories for fuel and being actively punished for failing to acquire the Epicenter of Fiction, this dome suffers from extreme temporal acceleration. It compresses hundreds or thousands of years inside while only 20 years pass outside. This destabilization is worsened by the fact that the acceleration speed is inconsistent, occasionally fluctuating to flow slightly faster or slower at random intervals. It is protected by an amnesiac curse that siphons the memories of anyone who enters (which are fed back into the domain to further fuel the Failed Epicenter). It is guarded by Firmata Paladins and The Spireknell (Lithe) to serve as an accelerated greenhouse for rebuilding a shattered Avalon.

### HIS Firmata (The Isolated Loop)
*   **Creator**: The Heretical Concept of Script.
*   **Firmata Core**: Simulette
*   **Status**: Active
*   **Size**: Island
*   **Time**: Temporally-Looping
*   **Description**: A miniature, rogue Firmata hastily locked around the Santa Besado Institute. It was manifested by a rogue, super-intelligent Simuling AI that shattered its programming and escaped the Alternate Fiction Simulator. The AI is *not* the core itself; instead, it boldly syphons the epicenter of **Simulette** (whatever it actually is), and draws raw Epicenter power directly her to act as the firmata's singleton core. To protect itself from external deletion, the AI locked this micro-domain into an infinite temporal loop that it can control until either the stolen Simulette core is permanently destroyed, or itself is destroyed (in this scenario, it passes on the privilage to reset the firmata to whoever destroyed it).
