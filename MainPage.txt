Scripted Fates is an overarching metaphysical Science-Fantasy project that spans across various mediums of story telling. From books and games, to roleplay servers and music videos. Each installment often follows the tales and struggles between mortals and divine, while puppet masters from beyond masquerade their theatrics from the shadows in an ontological proxy war.
