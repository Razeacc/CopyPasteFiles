# OVERVIEW
As of currently, Familiar Familiar is an anomaly even amongst the Phantcyians. It is the Embodiment of all pets and animals that have ceased to exist. However, being the most recently-forming Phantycian from Nul Detta, something that hasn't happened even before the Creation War, there's a major catch and problem with Familiar Familiar:
Not only does it pull anulled animal companion souls from fiction, but from *our* reality ad well. This gives Familiar Familiar a more "personal" connection to us that not, while Memetta and other Phantcyians are theoretically capable of, they actively choose to not use souls from our reality.

While Familiar Familiar's story tackles its journey and process in birthing itself as the newest Phantycian, it will also reveal why Phantycians actively restrict themselves to their layers of realitt

# ABILITIES
## Modus: Animalistic Shapeshift
> Familiar Familiar specializes in Shapeshifting into various animal forms and properties. This allows it to instinctively change its body to use the most appropriate animal feature on-instinct when performing certain actions.

## Modus: Writer's Block
> By metaphysically pressing itself down on a roleplayer's skribble, it can consume any actions the roleplayer sends to their character. The roleplayer would need to do something to get Familiar Familiar off them as to continue roleplaying normally.

## Epicenterhood: Shared Epicenter of Companionship
> Sharing with Crimmy, they're both capable of accessible and being aware of all companionships across fiction. However, due to Familiar Familiar also having souls from our world, this makes all those souls from our world become incompatible with the Epicenter here. (Only the Epicenter of Fiction can be held by anyone regardless of Reality Layer. All other Epicenters can only be processed by tbeir respectice layer).
And since Phantcyians often rely on Epicenterhood, having half of Familiar Familiar unable to process the Epicenter of Companionship heavily distorts its view on friendship and connection. It's akin to having dark blobs obscure half of your vision becausr half of your light cones are incompatible with the light of this world.
