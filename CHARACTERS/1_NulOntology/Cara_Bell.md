## Description
Cara Bell is a forlorn caradel, the first of its kind... or, at least, the first recorded one. Lithe and Phirmale (Morgale's Living Piece) made Cara Bell prior to The Spirepocalypse in preparation for the Memory Wipe.
Taking advantage of the sentience mechanics and their abundance of Avalonian Steel, they forged Cara Bell by splicing a modular Lejeseh Caradel with Avalonian Steel. This boosted its memory storage capabilities, at the cost of a sever reduction in sentience.
Currently, Cara Bell IS more sentient than a rock, but he is a lot LESS sentient than a worm, let alone other caradels. Its level of sentience is akin to that of a plant or coral, but far beneath things like anemone. He just has enough sentience to qualify as a Forlorn Caradel, letting Lithe use it as a weapon to store all his Caradeled-Archkin-Consumable Memories, which Cara Bell converts into Memoriam Tabs.
With a constant "sleeping" expression, Cara Bell is posed to genuinely not do anything throughout the entire story... unless someone somehow for whatever reason sprays blood on him, which may react with his innae Avalonian Steel composition. However, more likely than not, this will either leak Memoriam Tabs, or showcase the memories stored within, rather than bringing Cara Bell to life

## Appearance
> (This description uses many bell terminologies like crown, canons, clapper, yoke, etc.)
Looking like a rogue caradel head, his bell form's waist is shaped like a pyramid with a bell clapper inside him, which Lithe uses to ring and flow his abilities through. If anything, the clapper inside Cara Bell is more like a relay antenna than an actual clapper. Coincidentally though, this shape also vaguely resembles the logo use by archkin.
As for the yoke, it seems attached to his "crown/canons", which is merely the top point of his pyramid waist. This is where Lithe often grabs Cara Bell by hand, or ties a psyche link akin to an arcent.
Topping off his design, Cara Bell has two closed red eyes near the bottom of his form where the sound ring would be.

## Abilities
### Psyche Relay (Passive)
His clapper acts like a relay, being able to output signals, archose, and other magical abilites (frequencies) received by him from his crown and canon.
Meanwhile, his crown and canon acts as input receiver for magical abilities (frequencies). This means Cara Bell's user (Lithe) can send signals from himself to long range through Cara Bell. But the reverse is impossible, meaning people cannot attack Cara Bell to harm Lithe.

### Memoriam Tab Banker (Passive)
Utilizing his Avalonian Steel composition and Lejeseh Caradel origins, Cara Bell stores memories in the form of melodies. This allows him to arrange them in his mind more efficiently by connecting "motifs" together and saving space, alongside making it easier and faster for his Caradeled Archkin host (Lithe) to access and process them. It's akin to having an SSD compared to most Aberrant Caradels being HDDs, alongside also having File Compression AND pointers.
