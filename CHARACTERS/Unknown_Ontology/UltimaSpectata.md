## IDEA
A borderline joke character, it's in a vein similar to the Equilibrium Arbiter where it's an "Omniveral" Epicenter. Only in this case, it's an "Omniveral Metaphor of Spectators", if we were to use whatever from the Epicenter information was applicable to it.
Ultima Spectata, as his name suggests, is an omniveral spectator that jumps from reality level to another to witness and watch as much, and as many, fictions as possible.
He's akin to Gaunter o'Dimm from the Witcher games, but with a bit more of a commical twist.

There are two important factors to consider with Ultima. They don't interact or meddle with reality levels as much as possible by utilizing two properties:
The material, **Questionablium Canonininite**.
and
The location, **The Bobuverse**.

## Questionablium Canonininite
An energy-like material from whatever level of reality Ultimata originated from, it's a substance that acts akin to Shrodinger's Cat, but with a level of unbreakability. No matter what this material does, it will never leave a lasting effect, meaning it's always questionable whether or not whatever it does is "canon" in whenever level of reality it wad active in.
Aside from being used just like archose, it's also what its entire personal pocket plane is made of: The Bobuverse.

## The Bobuverse
Made entirely of Questionablium Canonininite, the Bobuverse is a questionably-canon alternative to the omniverse exclusive to Ultimata Spectata. It's what he uses to move between levels of reality and spectate them like TV screens. The reason for its name is completely unknown, even to Ultimata itself, but he swears it was not only named that, but also existed WAY before Ultimate himself.

As stated earlier, Ultimata is mostly a joke character, so it's unsure if he'll even appear in the future in any story. But one thing's (questionably) certain, he's always spectating. No matter what.
