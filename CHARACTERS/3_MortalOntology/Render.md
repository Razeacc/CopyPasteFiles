# Overview
A sentient sword made of Avalonian Steel with a humanoid body, Render was forged through the blasphemous union of Entropy and Creation. With a single slash, he's able to "render" a wave of "Impossible Matter (Endless Entropy)" when slashing. This entropy completely and entirely corrupts and overwrites a section of reality permanently. Anything caught within does not die, but becomes incapable of proceeding forward in their stories.
This ability is capable of bypassing even Plot Armor.
However, upon slashing once, he'll be instantly tried by The Court of The Fourth before being anulled from existence purely due to his ability to ignore even the narrative itself.

Render features barely enough sentience to say more than 2 words per sentence, and generally acts far more like an object than an actual person. He rarely showcases any opinions or wants, mostly since he cannot physically form them in the first place.

# History
## Avalonian-Yan'amese Origins
(Made by Morgale and Ouya. Morgale wanted a weapon against Sandatta Ulhysse, Ouya wanted a weapon against The Court of The Fourth. Both got banished/captured before Render could be completed.)

## Avalon Disaster
(He's eventually taken by the AoS and brought to Avalon City where they treat him like an object to be easier to control. However, upon Render doing absolutely nothing on his own to help during the Avalon Disaster attack from Al'Knoxus, the AoS shifted mindsets into wishing to treat Render more like a human.)

## Myriel
(The AoS eventually send Render to another sentient sword, Myriel, to go on an adventure together in hopes that her more human natures rubs off on Render. Meanwhile, the AoS work on renovating and upgrading Render's current living conditions in Avalon City... which is merely a single cabinet.)

## VS Ladie Luke
(If fate does not change his course, Render is destined to be wielded by the Vice President of the AoS to slash Ladie Luke atop Avalon .)
