## IDEA Overview
Ladie Luke, nicknamed "Lady Luck". She'll be a very privileged woman who was born and raised in Avalon City. Rbrt since her birth, not only was she taken into an adoptive family who spoiler her, she's faced almost next to little struggle her whole life.
As long as she put maximum effort and risk on the table, luck was on her side— no matter what. This caused her to not only grow up arrogant and greedy, but also hard-working and absolutely "brave". She always took on the hardest challenges with biggest rewards, because she knew she'd win every time.

## Equipmemt
Her main weapons involve a variety of equipment she won from Cubiqua city.
- (Magic) A pair of d20 dice, where 19 faces would instantly kill her, but a single face immediately harmed whoever she was targetting to deal "true" damage. 
- (AoE) Grenades she tosses into the air to release a volley of darts, with her under it as the "bullseye". She never gets hit but everyone around her does.
- (Ranged) A quick-reload quad-chambered revolver with a built-in 5th "dud" chamber, built specifically for high-stakes quick-succession Russian Roulette. She never gets shot herself, of course. When shooting, she always empties the chamber and includes herself in the targets.
- (Melee) A telepathic knife that was actually stolen from Kaitzar and modified for her own purposes. It causes others to materialize a knife in their hands (using one's own soul to guarantee "True" damage) when she grips it. While holding it, she then proceeds to do the "knife dance" with it onto her own hand, stabbing the blade between each finger in rapid succession, which forces the others to follow. Without enough alacrity, this attack can really cut up someone's hand.
- (Crowd Control) A poker chip sweeper she treats as both a "sword" and a "spear". Although it doesn't deal much damage, it's capable of moving people around almost telepathically, which she uses to control the environment to her advantage. Unlike all her other tools, this "weapon" was won during a death gamble against Pennypal Pincher himself, explaining why there's not much of a gamble with it— she's already paid her debt on this weapon.

After Ladie acquired most of her equipment, she set out to find out what happened with her parents. Due to her "luck", she managed to easily discover rumors and evidence showing that they visited the Equilibrium Arbiter before never being seen again. Ever since, her main goal would be to organize and manage her own group to take down the Epicenter of Equilibrium itself.

Organizing a faction "New Arch" by spreading her wealth and support to the impoverish in Avalon, she slowly formed a rebel group against the AoS that wasn't involved with the vicars whatsoever. However, whether she'd be a potential ally to characters like the Firmata Frontier Roleplayers one day, would depend entirely on their choices.


## Secrets
Before her birth, her parents made a deal with The Equilibrium Arbiter. They'd give up their relatively-impoverished lives for the Arbiter, who's the Epicenter of Balance, to make their daughter's life as "lucky as possible".
Her circumstance is similar to Tool, where they both have an ungodly-level of plot armor. But while Tool's psuedo immortality is due to his own skribble manipulating fate to give him plot armor— this woman's assistance is from the Equilibrium Arbiter.
This makes it quite ironic, that Lady Luck would be trying to take down the very source of her "luck". Despite that, even knowing her intentions, the Equilibrium Arbiter... for some reason, does absolutely nothing against it, even supportong it. Perhaps even it wants to see if it can take itself out.
