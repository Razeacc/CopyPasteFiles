# Overview
In order to attempt to become the proper Epicenter of Psyche, Nul Detta devised a massive overarching plan to cause a chain-reaction of several simultanious events all across The Worlds Aplenty.
The idea was to create a massive distraction for the AoS and Court of The Fourth. This would give them no time to actually investigate or deal with Nul Detta as he works through "hijacking" the Epicenter of Psyche: the soul of Khueen, who is still currently banished/annuled in The Null Crossing.
