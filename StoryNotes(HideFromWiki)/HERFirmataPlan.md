-   Morgale's Living Piece agreed to receive a tiny piece of Khueen, marking her as a candidate for "The Epicenter of Fiction"
-   Nul Detta and Morgale's Living Piece timed and waited for the perfect moment to create HER Firmata
-   The AoS attacked the golden spire in the middle of Lejos, turning it into a pale green spire and sending in the "reinforcements" from Avalon City: The Full-Scale Invasion of the AoS against An-Tri'Fhiana has begun
-   At the same time, Morgale's Living Piece began syphoning memories from everyone on The Continent
-   As this happens, Nul Detta cracks Khueen's soul while within The Null Crossing, shattering the Epicenter of Psyche and making Overwrites possible.
-   Morgale's Living Piece crosses the Epicenter of Fiction threshold and her soul is sent to The Court of The Fourth.
-   Morgale's Living Piece immediately taps into The Epicenter of Fiction. In that brief moment, she started creating HER Firmata before also immediately failing the trial.

-   Morgale's Living Piece splits herself using Overwrite, transfering half of her soul into HER Firmata while cutting herself off from Her Failed Epicenter physical body, letting the Failed Epicenter curse fully consume it.
-   HER Firmata is now established, the memories of everyone on The Continent, at the center of Lejos, has been taken away, Her Failed Epicenter is now active in the bottom of the Lunore Conservation Site, HIS Firmata is now active, and The Epicenter of Psyche is now damaged.
-   Spires all across The Worlds Aplenty readjust themselves to Lejos.
-   The Spirepocalypse has begun.
